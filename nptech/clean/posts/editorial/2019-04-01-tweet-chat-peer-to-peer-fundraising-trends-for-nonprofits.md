# [Tweet Chat] Peer-to-Peer Fundraising Trends for Nonprofits

> Please join us for a tweet chat on May 1st at 12 pm EDT to discuss peer-to-peer fundraising! Nonprofit Tech for Good (@nonprofitorgs) will be joined by co-host CrowdRise by GoFundMe (@crowdrise) to have a live tweet chat about the power of enabling your nonprofit’s supporters to fundraise on your organization’s behalf. During the tweet chat, we will share insights on how your supporters can create successful fundraising campaigns, use their personal stories and social networks to inspire giving, and how to convert these new one-time donors into dependable regular donors. Please join us for a tweet chat on May 1st at 12 pm EDT to discuss peer-to-peer fundraising! Nonprofit Tech for Good ([**@nonprofitorgs**](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs)) will be joined by co-host **[CrowdRise by GoFundMe](https://www.crowdrise.com/)** ([**@crowdrise**](https://twitter.com/crowdrise)) to have a live tweet chat about the power of enabling your nonprofit’s supporters to fundraise on your organization’s behalf. During the tweet chat, we will share insights on how your supporters can create successful fundraising campaigns, use their personal stories and social networks to inspire giving, and how to convert these new one-time donors into dependable regular donors.

## **How to Participate**

At 12 pm EST on May 1st, participate or follow the discussion on Twitter at [**#P2PFundraising**](https://twitter.com/hashtag/p2pfundraising):

1. Ask questions or share insights about your experience with peer-to-peer fundraising during the tweet chat by using the hashtag, **[#P2PFundraising](https://twitter.com/hashtag/p2pfundraising)**.
2. Reply to comments and questions posted during the tweet chat to share resources and expertise or ask follow-up questions.
3. Retweet what you’d like to share with your followers.

## **RSVP**

[**Please RSVP for the tweet chat on Facebook**](https://www.facebook.com/events/2378533645531841/) to let us know that you’ll be joining us on May 1st or [**sign up for an email reminder**](https://fundraising.crowdrise.com/tweet-chat-p2p-fundraising-trends?) for the #P2PFundraising tweet chat. You’ll receive the email from CrowdRise by GoFundMe on the morning of the chat as a reminder to tune in.
