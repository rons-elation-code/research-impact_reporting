# Online Stores by Nonprofits

## 1) ACLU Store :: [shop.aclu.org](https://shop.aclu.org/)

Proceeds benefit the American Civil Liberties Union, a nonprofit that works to defend the rights and liberties guaranteed by the Constitution and laws of the United States.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/ACLU-Shop.jpg?resize=600%2C370)](https://shop.aclu.org/)

## 2) Amnesty International Store :: [shop.amnestyusa.org](https://shop.amnestyusa.org/)

Proceeds benefit Amnesty International, a nonprofit that works for the human rights of all people – no matter who they are or where they are.

## 3) Animals Asia Shop :: [shop.animalsasia.org](https://shop.animalsasia.org)

Proceeds benefit Animals Asia work to end bear bile farming and improving the welfare of animals in China and Vietnam.

## 4) Art Institute of Chicago Museum Shop :: [shop.artic.edu](https://shop.artic.edu)

The Art Institute of Chicago Museum Shop sells unique and beautiful objects from around the world in support of the arts.

## 5) Best Friends Animal Society Store :: [shop.bestfriends.org](https://shop.bestfriends.org/)

Proceeds benefit the Best Friends Animal Society, a nonprofit working to end the killing in America’s animal shelters through adoption, spay and neuter education, and no-kill initiatives.

## 6) Field Museum Store :: [store.fieldmuseum.org](https://store.fieldmuseum.org/)

The Field Museum Store offers gifts for those interested in science and cultural, natural, and human history.

## 7) First Book Marketplace :: [fbmarketplace.org](https://www.fbmarketplace.org/)

Proceeds benefit First Book, a nonprofit that provides new books and educational resources to schools and programs in low-income communities.

## 8) Getty Store :: [shop.getty.edu](https://shop.getty.edu/)

The Getty Store enhances knowledge and appreciation of the arts by providing unique high-quality merchandise and books that reflect the Getty Museum as a whole.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Getty-Store.jpg?resize=600%2C410)](https://shop.getty.edu/)

## 9) Global Goods Partners :: [globalgoodspartners.org](https://globalgoodspartners.org)

Global Goods Partners have partnered with over 60 artisan groups in more than 20 countries throughout Asia, Africa, and the Americas to help bring to market the fair trade, handcrafted products they produce.

## 10) Human Rights Campaign Nonprofit Store :: [shop.hrc.org](https://shop.hrc.org/)

100% of net proceeds go directly to support the work of HRC to improve the lives of LGBTQ Americans.

## 11) MoMa Design Store :: [store.moma.org](https://store.moma.org/)

Classic modern and cutting edge design products for adults and kids, including items produced exclusively for the Museum of Modern Art (New York).

## 12) National Park Foundation Gift Shop :: [nationalparks.org/gift-shop](https://www.nationalparks.org/support/gift-shop)

The National Park Foundation offer their supporters a great way to show their love for the national parks – while supporting them at the same time.

## 13) NPR Shop :: [shop.npr.org](https://shop.npr.org/)

Every purchase supports NPR programming by purchasing NPR-branded clothing, accessories, and gifts.

## 14) National Wildlife Federation Store :: [shopnwf.org](https://www.shopnwf.org/index.jsp)

Purchases support the NWF’s mission to ensure wildlife thrive in a rapidly changing world.

## 15) PBS Kids Shop :: [shop.pbskids.org](https://shop.pbskids.org)

From books and DVDs to toys and party supplies, playtime has never been more educational and fun.

## 16) PETA Shop :: [shop.peta.org](https://shop.peta.org/)

All profits from merchandise sales go directly toward supporting PETA’s work for animals.

## 17) Serrv International :: [serrv.org](https://www.serrv.org/)

Serrv International is a nonprofit dedicated to fighting global poverty through fair and ethical trade.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Serrv.jpg?resize=600%2C442)](https://www.serrv.org/)

## 18) SFMOMA Museum Store :: [museumstore.sfmoma.org](https://museumstore.sfmoma.org)

The SFMOMA Museum Store offers the West Coast’s finest selection of modern and contemporary art books, as well as innovative design objects and furnishings, contemporary jewelry and apparel, toys, and posters and stationery.

## 19) Sierra Club Store :: [store.sierraclub.org](https://store.sierraclub.org/storefront.aspx)

All proceeds go towards helping the Sierra Club promote the responsible use of the earth’s ecosystems and resources.

## 20) St. Jude Gift Shop :: [giftshop.stjude.org](https://giftshop.stjude.org/)

One-of-a-kind gifts designed to help St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital continue to treat and defeat childhood cancer and other deadly diseases.

## 21) Surfrider Foundation Store :: [shop.surfrider.org](https://shop.surfrider.org)

100% of profits help fund Surfrider’s mission to  protect the world’s ocean, waves and beaches. Products are sustainable and environmentally-friendly.

## 22) Survival Shop :: [shop.survivalinternational.org](https://shop.survivalinternational.org/)

All profits go towards helping Survival International fight for the survival of tribal peoples worldwide.

## 23) Ten Thousand Villages :: [tenthousandvillages.com](https://www.tenthousandvillages.com)

Shop for unique, handmade gifts from around the world including fair trade baskets, jewelry, and crafts from artisans located in nearly 40 countries.

## 24) UNICEF Market :: [market.unicefusa.org](https://www.market.unicefusa.org)

Proceeds go to both the talented artisans that create the beautifully crafted products in the UNICEF Market and to help UNICEF save and protect children.

## 25) World Wildlife Fund Gifts :: [gifts.worldwildlife.org](https://gifts.worldwildlife.org/gift-center/)

Help create a safer world for wildlife, protect amazing places, and build a future where people live in harmony with nature by purchasing a gift from WWF’s Gift Center.
