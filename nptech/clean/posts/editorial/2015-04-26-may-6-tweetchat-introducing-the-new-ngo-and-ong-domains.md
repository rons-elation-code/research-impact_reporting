After four years of planning and hard work, on May 6 the Public Interest Registry and OnGood will release the new .NGO and .ONG domains worldwide. This tweetchat will highlight three reasons why your NGO/nonprofit,/charity should register the new domains and  discuss how you can integrate the new domains into your online branding effort.

### **Three Ways to Participate:**

1. Ask questions live during the tweetchat by using the [#**NGOtechchat**](https://twitter.com/search?f=realtime&q=%23NGOtechchat&src=typd) hashtag.

2.  Share your thoughts and expertise by “Replying” to comments and questions posted during the tweetchat.

3. Retweet information and advice that you think is useful to your followers.

### **Sign up to receive an #NGOtechchat email alert:**

To be sent a #NGOtechchat reminder on the morning of the tweet chat, please subscribe to the **[OnGood email list](https://www.ongood.ngo/portal/)** and/or **[RSVP on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/events/1558311224433119/)**.
