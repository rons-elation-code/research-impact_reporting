# 1. Reach [reachmobile.no](http://www.reachmobile.no)

The Reach app encourages users to explore the world by sharing photos and videos of their travels or visits to local landmarks and natural sites. Engaging with ads earns users Recoins that can then be donated to their favorite nonprofits.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Reach.jpg?resize=600%2C292)](http://www.reachmobile.no)

# 2. Stance [takeastance.us](http://takeastance.us)

Stance delivers voice messages straight to a congressperson’s voicemail and if he or she’s voicemail is full, Stance keeps calling until it’s available.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/STANCE.jpg?resize=600%2C346)](http://takeastance.us)

# 3. MaximusLife [maximuslife.com](http://www.maximuslife.com/challenges/)

Join a MaximusLife Challenge to walk 10,000 steps a day, give up salt for a week, do yoga three times weekly, etc. and if you succeed, a donation is made to charity on your behalf.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/maximus.jpg?resize=600%2C338)](http://www.maximuslife.com/challenges/)

# 4. Gone for Good [goneforgood.org.uk](https://goneforgood.org.uk)

Take a photo of your unwanted goods, schedule a pick up, and a charity will collect your goods directly from your door.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Gone-for-Good.jpg?resize=600%2C349)](https://goneforgood.org.uk)

# 5. GiveTide [givetide.com](https://www.givetide.com)

Users add their credit card info to the app, GiveTide rounds up all transactions to the nearest dollar, and then users give the money raised to their favorite nonprofits with a single tap.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/GiveTide2.jpg?resize=600%2C358)](https://www.givetide.com)

# 6. DriveSafe [thedrivesafeapp.com](http://www.thedrivesafeapp.com)

The DriveSafe app sets up auto-responders for messaging apps and texting and turns on automatically as soon as you get in your car.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/drivesafe.jpg?resize=600%2C344)](http://www.thedrivesafeapp.com)
