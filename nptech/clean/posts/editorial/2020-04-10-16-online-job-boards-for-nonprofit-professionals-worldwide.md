*The bad news?* Tens of millions are out of work worldwide due to the COVID-19 crisis. *The good news?* The economy will eventually bounce back and a record number of new employment opportunities will be available and many of these future jobs will be remote. Nonprofits that embrace the flexible work movement [**experience many benefits**](https://www.zippia.com/employer/8-benefits-allowing-employees-work-remotely/), such as increased productivity and retention and decreased expenses and pollution contribution. Win-win. Bookmark your browsers! Your next dream job is coming soon.

## 1) Work for Good :: [workforgood.org](https://www.workforgood.org/)

Since our founding in 1999 as Opportunity Knocks, one of the first job boards focused exclusively on the mission-driven careers, our team at Work for Good has helped more than 30,000 purpose-driven organizations find the talent they need.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Work-For-Good.jpg?resize=960%2C635)](https://www.workforgood.org/)

## 2) United Nations Jobs (Worldwide) :: [unjobs.org](http://www.unjobs.org/)

A comprehensive aggregator of job openings at United Nations offices worldwide.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/UNJOBS.jpg?resize=960%2C618)](https://unjobs.org/)

## 3) Third Sector Jobs (UK) :: [jobs.thirdsector.co.uk](http://jobs.thirdsector.co.uk/)

Third Sector Jobs is a leading job site for hundreds of the latest charity jobs, voluntary jobs, and fundraising jobs in the United Kingdom.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Third-Sector-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C586)](https://jobs.thirdsector.co.uk/)

## 4) Pro Bono Australia :: [probonoaustralia.com.au/jobs](https://probonoaustralia.com.au/jobs/)

Pro Bono Australia provide news, jobs and resources for the common good.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Pro-Bono-Australia.jpg?resize=960%2C575)](https://probonoaustralia.com.au/jobs/)

## 5) Philanthropy News Digest (US) :: [philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs](http://philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs)

PND’s job board provides listings of current full-time job openings at tax-exempt organizations. Tax-exempt organizations may submit up to ten postings a month, free of charge.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/PND-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C569)](http://philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs)

## 6) NTEN Job Board (US) :: [nten.org/jobs](https://www.nten.org/jobs/)

NTEN posts full-time nonprofit tech jobs and short-term opportunities at organizations working for social change.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/NTEN-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C622)](https://www.nten.org/jobs/)

## 7) Nonprofit Career Network (US) :: [nonprofitcareer.com](https://www.nonprofitcareer.com/)

For people who want to participate in the nonprofit or Impact Business sector and the organizations that have a need for those people.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Nonprofit-Career-Network.jpg?resize=960%2C627)](https://www.nonprofitcareer.com/)

## 8) National Council of Nonprofits (US) :: [careers.councilofnonprofits.org](https://careers.councilofnonprofits.org/)

Founded in 1990, the National Council of Nonprofits is a trusted resource and advocate for America’s charitable nonprofits.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/National-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C676)](https://careers.councilofnonprofits.org/)

## 9) Idealist (Worldwide) :: [idealist.org](http://www.idealist.org/)

Idealist connect Idealists—people who want to do good—with employment and volunteer opportunities for action all over the world. The website is available in **[English](https://www.idealist.org/)**, [**Portuguese,**](https://www.idealist.org/pt) and [**Spanish**](https://www.idealist.org/es).

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Idealist-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C539)](https://www.idealist.org/en)

## 10) Foundation List (US) :: [foundationlist.org](https://www.foundationlist.org/)

Positions actively posted and recruited for on Foundation List include part-time and full-time positions from executive to entry level.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Foundation-List.jpg?resize=960%2C577)](https://www.foundationlist.org/)

## 11) DevNetJobs.org (Worldwide) :: [devnetjobs.org](http://www.devnetjobs.org/)

DevNetJobs.org agreegates international development jobs and consulting opportunities worldwide.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/DevNetJobs.jpg?resize=943%2C628)](http://www.devnetjobs.org/)

## 12) Devex (Worldwide) :: [devex.com/jobs](https://www.devex.com/jobs)

Devex is the media platform and jobs board for the global development community.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Devex-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C564)

## 13) Chronicle of Philanthropy (US) :: [jobs.philanthropy.com](https://jobs.philanthropy.com/)

A extensive job board and career resource center for nonprofit professionals and foundation executives.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Chronicle-of-Philanthropy.jpg?resize=960%2C620)](https://jobs.philanthropy.com/)

## 14) Charity Village (Canada) :: [charityvillage.com](https://charityvillage.com/search/#results/5e908bdcc752fbe89d083394?cv_volunteer_pos=exclude&page_num=1&kw=)

For more than 20 years, CharityVillage has helped thousands of Canadian charities find exceptional talent.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Charity-Village.jpg?resize=960%2C695)](https://charityvillage.com/search/)

## 15) Charity Digital Jobs (UK) :: [charitydigitaljobs.org](https://charitydigitaljobs.org/)

Charity Digital Jobs connects UK charities with the best digital talent in the UK.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Charity-Digital-Jobs.jpg?resize=960%2C691)](https://charitydigitaljobs.org/jobs/)

## 16) Bridgespan Group (US) :: [bridgespan.org/jobs](https://www.bridgespan.org/jobs/nonprofit-jobs/nonprofit-job-board)

The Bridgespan Group is a leading social impact consultancy for nonprofits and NGOs, philanthropists, and investors that also offers an online job board.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Bridgespan-Group.jpg?resize=960%2C785)
