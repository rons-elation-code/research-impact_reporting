# For U.S. Job Seekers

##### 1) Chronicle of Philanthropy :: [jobs.philanthropy.com](https://jobs.philanthropy.com/)

A extensive job board and career resource center for nonprofit professionals and foundation executives.

##### 2) Foundation List :: [foundationlist.org](https://www.foundationlist.org/)

Positions actively posted and recruited for on Foundation List include part-time and full-time positions from executive to entry-level.

##### 3) National Council of Nonprofits :: [careers.councilofnonprofits.org](https://careers.councilofnonprofits.org/)

Founded in 1990, the National Council of Nonprofits is a trusted resource and advocate for America’s charitable nonprofits.

##### 4) Philanthropy News Digest :: [philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs](https://philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs)

PND’s job board provides listings of current full-time job openings at U.S. nonprofits, foundations, and institutions of higher education.

##### 5) Work for Good :: [workforgood.org](https://www.workforgood.org/)

Since their founding in 1999 as Opportunity Knocks, one of the first job boards focused exclusively on mission-driven careers, Work for Good has helped more than 30,000 purpose-driven organizations find the talent they need.

# For Job Seekers Worldwide

##### 6) Charity Village (Canada) :: [charityvillage.com](https://charityvillage.com/)

For more than 20 years, CharityVillage has helped thousands of Canadian charities find exceptional talent.

##### 7) Devex (Worldwide) :: [devex.com/jobs](https://www.devex.com/jobs)

Devex is the media platform and jobs board for the global development community.

##### 8) Idealist (Worldwide) :: [idealist.org](http://www.idealist.org/)

Idealist connect Idealists—people who want to do good—with employment and volunteer opportunities for action all over the world. The website is available in **[English](https://www.idealist.org/)**, [**Portuguese,**](https://www.idealist.org/pt) and [**Spanish**](https://www.idealist.org/es).

##### 9) Pro Bono Australia :: [probonoaustralia.com.au/jobs](https://probonoaustralia.com.au/jobs/)

Pro Bono Australia provides news, jobs, and resources for the common good.

##### 10) Third Sector Jobs (UK) :: [jobs.thirdsector.co.uk](http://jobs.thirdsector.co.uk/)

Third Sector Jobs is a leading job site for hundreds of the latest charity jobs, voluntary jobs, and fundraising jobs in the United Kingdom.
