> Thanks to MobileCause, I have a new text messaging service to explore and experiment with. If you are interested in learning about how to utilize text messaging and the Mobile Web for your nonprofit, then please text NONPROFITORGS to 51555 (or enter your phone here) to subscribe to receive text alerts from Nonprofit Tech 2.0. **[![textmessaging](http://nonprofitorgs.files.wordpress.com/2013/04/textmessaging.jpg?w=300&resize=300%2C228)](http://nonprofitorgs.files.wordpress.com/2013/04/textmessaging.jpg)**

Thanks to [MobileCause](http://www.mobilecause.com/), I have a new text messaging service to explore and experiment with. If you are interested in learning about how to utilize text messaging and the Mobile Web for your nonprofit, then please **text NONPROFITORGS to 51555** **([or enter your phone here](http://www.diosacommunications.com/signupmobilealerts.html))** to subscribe to receive text alerts from Nonprofit Tech 2.0.

Text alerts will be sent 2-3 times monthly and will:

- Promote nonprofits and nonprofit mobile campaigns
- Share resources and best practices on how nonprofits can utilize the Mobile Web
- Feature breaking news relevant to the nonprofit sector

**\* Currently only available to U.S. mobile subscribers. Message and Data Rates May Apply. Simply text STOP to 51555 to unsubscribe at any time. Text **HELP** to 51555 for help. Terms: [igfn.org/t](http://igfn.org/t). Privacy Policy: [igfn.org/p](http://igfn.org/p).**
