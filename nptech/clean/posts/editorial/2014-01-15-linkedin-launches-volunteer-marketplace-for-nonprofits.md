# LinkedIn Launches Volunteer Marketplace for Nonprofits

> To help nonprofits recruit qualified volunteers and to assist individuals eager to volunteer with nonprofits, LinkedIn has launched a Volunteer Marketplace where nonprofits can post volunteer opportunities and LinkedIn members can search for volunteer opportunities in their local communities. According to LinkedIn for Good, 82% of surveyed LinkedIn members have communicated that they want volunteer their time and skills and thus far more than 600,000 LinkedIn members have added their Volunteer Experience & Causes to their LinkedIn profiles. [![Volunteering-Is-Good-for-Your-Career-LinkedIn-Nonprofits-final](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Volunteering-Is-Good-for-Your-Career-LinkedIn-Nonprofits-final-300x300.jpg?resize=300%2C300)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Volunteering-Is-Good-for-Your-Career-LinkedIn-Nonprofits-final.jpg)To help nonprofits recruit qualified volunteers and to assist individuals eager to volunteer with nonprofits, LinkedIn has launched a [Volunteer Marketplace](http://volunteer.linkedin.com/index.html) where nonprofits can post volunteer opportunities and LinkedIn members can search for volunteer opportunities in their local communities. According to [LinkedIn for Good](http://www.linkedin.com/lifg), 82% of surveyed LinkedIn members have communicated that they want volunteer their time and skills and thus far more than 600,000 LinkedIn members have added their [Volunteer Experience & Causes](http://www.linkedin.com/profile/edit-volunteering-combined?trk=Vol_LIFG_Prof_Exp) to their LinkedIn profiles.

The service is not free for nonprofits, but it is offered at a discounted rate of 90 percent off LinkedIn’s standard rate for job postings, thus $19.50 for a 30-day volunteer posting. To begin, your nonprofit must first claim and set up your [LinkedIn Company Page](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2013/07/14/ten-must-follow-nonprofits-on-linkedin/) and then visit [LinkedIn for Nonprofits](http://nonprofit.linkedin.com/index.html) to post your volunteer opportunities:
