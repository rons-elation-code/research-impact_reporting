# 14 Job Boards for Nonprofit Professionals Worldwide

> The nonprofit job market is tightening primarily due to a loss in federal grants and donors giving less in response to economic instability. If you are a nonprofit professional in need of new employment, this post features 14 of the top nonprofit job boards.

The U.S. nonprofit job market is tightening primarily due to a loss in federal grants and donors giving less in response to economic instability. Add to that the continued sprawl of AI and automation worldwide and the decline in international aid, 2026 could be a tough year for do-gooders across the globe.

If you are a nonprofit professional in need of new employment, below are 14 of the top nonprofit job boards. To grow your skill set and quickly improve your resume and LinkedIn profile, [Nonprofit Tech for Good offers three certificate programs](https://www.nptechforgood.com/certificate-programs/) for nonprofit professionals—and when the time comes to negotiate your new salary, learn from [Idealist’s Guide to Nonprofit Salaries](https://www.idealist.org/en/careers/nonprofit-salaries-guide).

# For U.S. Job Seekers

**1) Work for Good :: [workforgood.org](https://www.workforgood.org/)**

Since its founding in 1999 as Opportunity Knocks, one of the first job boards focused exclusively on mission-driven careers, Work for Good has helped more than 30,000 purpose-driven organizations find the talent they need.

**2) Philanthropy News Digest :: [philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs](https://philanthropynewsdigest.org/jobs)**

PND’s job board provides listings of current full-time job openings at U.S. nonprofits, foundations, and institutions of higher education.

**3) National Nonprofits :: [nationalnonprofits.org](https://nationalnonprofits.org/)**

A nonprofit job board that features management positions with reputable organizations that also offers an extensive set of recruitment tools for nonprofits.

**4) National Council of Nonprofits :: [careers.councilofnonprofits.org](https://careers.councilofnonprofits.org/)**

Founded in 1990, the National Council of Nonprofits is a trusted resource and advocate for America’s charitable nonprofits.

**5) LinkedIn :: [linkedin.com/jobs/nonprofit-jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/nonprofit-jobs)**

LinkedIn has an extensive job board that offers filtering of available positions by salary and on-site/remote/hybrid and provides an “Easy Apply” option.

**6) Foundation List :: [foundationlist.org](https://www.foundationlist.org/)**

Positions posted and recruited for on Foundation List include part-time and full-time employment from executive to entry-level.

**7) FlexJobs :: [flexjobs.com](https://www.flexjobs.com/)**

Founded in 2007, FlexJobs lists remote and flexible job opportunities in the nonprofit sector.

**8) ExecSearches.com :: [execsearches.com](https://execsearches.com/)**

The ExecSearches.com job board has been serving the nonprofit community since 1999.

**9) Chronicle of Philanthropy :: [jobs.philanthropy.com](https://jobs.philanthropy.com/)**

An extensive job board and career resource center for nonprofit professionals and foundation executives.

## For Job Seekers Worldwide

**10) Pro Bono Australia :: [probonoaustralia.niceboard.co](https://probonoaustralia.niceboard.co/)**

Pro Bono Australia provides news, jobs, and resources for the common good.

**11) Idealist (Worldwide) :: [idealist.org](http://www.idealist.org/)**

Idealist connect Idealists—people who want to do good—with employment and volunteer opportunities for action all over the world. The website is also available in [**Portuguese**](https://www.idealist.org/pt) and [**Spanish**](https://www.idealist.org/es).

**12) Devex (Worldwide) :: [devex.com/jobs](https://www.devex.com/jobs)**

Devex is the media platform and jobs board for the global development community.

**13) Charity Village (Canada) :: [charityvillage.com](https://charityvillage.com/)**

For more than 20 years, CharityVillage has helped thousands of Canadian charities find exceptional talent.

**14) CharityJob (United Kingdom):: [charityjob.co.uk](https://www.charityjob.co.uk/)**

CharityJob is the UK’s largest and most specialized job board carrying non-profit, NGO, social enterprise, CIC, and voluntary jobs.
