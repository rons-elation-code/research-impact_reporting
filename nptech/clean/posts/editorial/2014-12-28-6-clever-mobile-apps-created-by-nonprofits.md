# 6 Clever Mobile Apps Created by Nonprofits

The hard truth is that only 39% of mobile apps are opened 11 times or more and 20% of apps are only opened once. That said, the rapid sales of smartphones worldwide ensure a growing a mobile app audience in coming years, but for nonprofit mobile apps to be successful they must be well-designed and useful. ## 1. United Nations Calendar of Observances

The UN Calendar of Observances app provides details of UN observances as well as ways to get involved in related events.  
**[iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/calendar-observances-making/id579193062) | [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.un.mobile.calendar&hl=en) | [United Nations](https://unp.un.org/calendar/)**

[![UN APP Google Play](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/UN-APP-Google-Play.png?resize=600%2C268)](https://unp.un.org/calendar/)

## 2. SafeNight

When someone is in urgent need of safe shelter to escape domestic violence, SafeNight enables users to make an immediate donation to pay for a hotel room for the person in need.  
**[iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/US/app/id835672659) | [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.caravan.safenight&hl=en) | [Windows Phone](http://www.windowsphone.com/en-us/store/app/safenight/9bfaadea-2476-461a-b751-f80a8211849c) | [Caravan Studios](http://www.caravanstudios.org/#!safenight/cfvg)**

[![safe night google play](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/safe-night-google-play.png?resize=600%2C272)](http://www.caravanstudios.org/#!safenight/cfvg)

## 3. iCitzen

iCitizen is an app that enables users to keep track of political issues and voice their opinions and thoughts directly to elected officials.  
**[iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/icitizen-for-iphone/id725655449) | [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.icitizen) | [HeadCount](http://blog.icitizen.com/icitizen-and-headcount-announce-partnership-to-enhance-civic-engagement-and-citizen-involvement-in-government)**

[![icitizen app google play](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/icitizen-app-google-play.png?resize=600%2C268)](http://blog.icitizen.com/icitizen-and-headcount-announce-partnership-to-enhance-civic-engagement-and-citizen-involvement-in-government)

## 4. Food Scores

Environmental Working Group’s Food Scores app is a consumers guide to healthy, affordable food that’s good for people and the planet.  
**[iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/ewg-food-scores/id930172079) | [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ewg.foodscores&hl=en) | [Environmental Working Group](http://www.ewg.org/foodscores)**

[![Food Score google play](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Food-Score-google-play.png?resize=600%2C271)](http://www.ewg.org/foodscores)

## 5. Charity Navigator

This app helps guide intelligent giving by providing ratings for nearly 7,000 charities based on their financial health, accountability and transparency.  
**[iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/charity-navigator/id740191196) | [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.charitynavigator.mobile&hl=en) | [Charity Navigator](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/charity-navigator/id740191196)**

[![Charity Naviagor Google Play](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Charity-Naviagor-Google-Play.png?resize=600%2C271)](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/charity-navigator/id740191196)

## 6. Blood Donor

This app enables users to find nearby Red Cross blood drives, schedule appointments, and earn rewards from premier retailers.  
**[iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/blood-donor-by-american-red/id911428916) | [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.cube.arc.blood) | [Windows Phone](http://www.windowsphone.com/en-us/store/app/safenight/9bfaadea-2476-461a-b751-f80a8211849c) | [Red Cross](http://www.redcross.org/mobile-apps/blood-app)**

[![red cross app google play](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/red-cross-app-google-play.png?resize=600%2C278)](http://www.redcross.org/mobile-apps/blood-app)
