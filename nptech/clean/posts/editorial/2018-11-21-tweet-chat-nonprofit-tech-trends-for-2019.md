Join us for a tweet chat on January 16 at 12pm EST to discuss nonprofit tech trends in the new year! Nonprofit Tech for Good will be co-hosting a tweet chat with [**Wired Impact**](https://wiredimpact.com/) to lead a discussion on nonprofit tech trends, such as website design, social media fundraising, email marketing, privacy, and web accessibility.

## **How to Participate**

At 12pm EST on January 16, participate or follow the discussion on Twitter at [**#nptech19**](https://twitter.com/hashtag/nptech19?f=tweets):

1. Ask questions or share relevant information during the tweet chat by using the hashtag, [**#nptech19**](https://twitter.com/hashtag/nptech19?f=tweets).
2. Reply to comments and questions posted during the tweet chat to share your thoughts or ask follow-up questions.
3. Retweet what you’d like to share with your followers.
