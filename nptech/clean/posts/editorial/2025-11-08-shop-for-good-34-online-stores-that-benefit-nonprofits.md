# Shop for Good: 34 Online Stores That Benefit Nonprofits 🎁

> The National Retail Federation’s annual holiday forecast predicts retail sales in November and December will grow between 3.7% and 4.2% over 2024. By shopping these 34 online stores, socially conscious consumers can channel some of their spending power this holiday season into the nonprofit sector and the Greater Good.

The [**National Retail Federation’s**](https://nrf.com/media-center/press-releases/nrf-predicts-highest-holiday-retail-sales-record) annual holiday forecast predicts retail sales in November and December will grow between 3.7% and 4.2% over 2024. By shopping at any of the 34 online stores listed below, socially conscious consumers can channel some of their spending power this holiday season into the nonprofit sector and the Greater Good. For those who prefer to donate money as gifts, consider purchasing a charity gift card from **[TisBest.org](https://www.tisbest.org/)** or **[GlobalGiving.org](https://www.globalgiving.org/gifts/)**.

---

## 1) World Wildlife Fund Gifts :: [gifts.wwf.org](https://gifts.worldwildlife.org/gift-center/)

Help create a safer world for wildlife and protect amazing places by purchasing a gift from WWF’s Gift Center.

## 2) UNICEF Market :: [market.unicefusa.org](https://www.market.unicefusa.org)

Proceeds go to the artisans who create the beautifully crafted products and to help UNICEF save and protect children worldwide.

## 3) To Write Love on Her Here Arms Store :: [store.twloha.com](https://store.twloha.com/)

Shop the nonprofit TWLOHA store to support hope and help for people struggling with depression, addiction, self-injury, and suicide.

## 4) Ten Thousand Villages :: [tenthousandvillages.com](https://www.tenthousandvillages.com)

Shop for unique, handmade gifts from around the world including fair trade baskets, jewelry, and crafts from artisans located in nearly 40 countries.

## 5) Sweet Relief Musicans Fund :: [sweet-relief-musicians-fund.myshopify.com](https://sweet-relief-musicians-fund.myshopify.com/)

Proceeds aid musicians and music industry workers who struggle with illness, disease, and age-related problems.

## 6) Survival Shop :: [shop.survivalinternational.org](https://shop.survivalinternational.org/)

All profits go towards helping Survival International fight for the survival of tribal peoples worldwide.

## 7) Surfrider Foundation Store :: [shop.surfrider.org](https://shop.surfrider.org)

100% of profits help fund Surfrider’s mission to protect the world’s ocean, waves, and beaches. Products are sustainable and environmentally-friendly.

## 8) St. Jude Gift Shop :: [giftshop.stjude.org](https://giftshop.stjude.org/)

One-of-a-kind gifts designed to help St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital continue to treat and defeat childhood cancer and other deadly diseases.

## 9) Sierra Club Store :: [store.sierraclub.org](https://store.sierraclub.org/storefront.aspx)

All proceeds go towards helping the Sierra Club promote the responsible use of the earth’s ecosystems and resources.

## 10) SFMOMA Museum Store :: [museumstore.sfmoma.org](https://museumstore.sfmoma.org)
