# How to Create a Twitter Moment for Your Nonprofit

> Twitter Moments as defined by Twitter are curated tweets collected into a story. First launched in October 2015 in partnership with a select few media partners, as of September 29 all Twitter users can now create Moments. For nonprofits active on Twitter, Moments are new way to showcase the best tweets about your event or campaign. Moments are meant predominately for live reporting and mobile consumption. You’ll either have a Moment that looks great in Mobile (vertical images and videos) or a Moment better viewed on a desktop (square and rectangle images in full size). For the International Fundraising Congress 2016 Moment

For the **[International Fundraising Congress 2016 Moment](https://twitter.com/i/moments/789024560532291584)** desktop was prioritized. If possible, view the Moment on both desktop and mobile before experimenting with creating your own Moment. That said, creating Moments has a learning curve. Use the nine steps steps detailed below to guide your nonprofit when creating its first Twitter Moment.

# 1. Go to Moments > Create new Moment.

# 5. Add tweets to your moment by selecting tweets (✔️) you have tweeted or liked or by conducting a search.

# 6. Rearrange tweets in your preferred order by selecting up or down arrows.

[![moments-8](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Moments-8.png?resize=600%2C445)](https://twitter.com/i/moments/789024560532291584)

# [spacer2](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Spacer2.jpg)

# 7. When finished, select Publish. You will then be prompted to crop photos for mobile. You can crop all photos at once with one click or select each photo individually. Only crop images that are compatible with mobile.
