# 2018 Global Trends in Giving Report

Based upon the survey results of 6,057 donors worldwide, the [**2018 Global Trends in Giving Report**](http://givingreport.ngo) summarizes donor data across six continents about how online and mobile technology effects giving. The report also examines the impact of gender, generation, ideology, religion, and donor size upon giving and volunteerism.
