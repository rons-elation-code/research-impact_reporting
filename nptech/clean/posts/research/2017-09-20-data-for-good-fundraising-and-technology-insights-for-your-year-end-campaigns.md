# Data for Good: Fundraising and Technology Insights for your Year-End Campaigns

> Presented on September 20 via webinar, the slides below highlight data from the 2017 Global NGO Technology Report, the 2017 Global Trends in Giving Report, and Network for Good’s donor platform to determine if U.S. nonprofits are using technology in the ways that their donors and supporters prefer. Attendees were be given access to exclusive U.S. donor data and data-based analysis of how effectively nonprofits are using online communication and fundraising tools. To register for future free webinars, please see the Nonprofit Tech for Good Webinar Schedule.
