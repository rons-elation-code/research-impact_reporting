# 1. In 2014, 1.4 billion people worldwide donated money to nonprofit organizations.

[![PIR-7](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/PIR-7.jpg?resize=600%2C600)](https://www.ongood.ngo/portal/facts-and-stats-about-ngos-worldwide)**[Source: OnGood](https://www.ongood.ngo/portal/facts-and-stats-about-ngos-worldwide)**

# 2. 12% of all giving happens in the last three days of the year.

[![most donations made at the end of the year 2](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/most-donations-made-at-the-end-of-the-year-2.png?resize=600%2C469)](http://www1.networkforgood.org/digitalgivingindex)**[Source: Network for Good](http://www1.networkforgood.org/digitalgivingindex)**

# 3. 64% of all donations are made by women.
