# @NonprofitOrgs to Report Live from #14NTC

> Through a partnership with the Public Interest Registry, Heather Mansfield of Nonprofit Tech for Good will be attending the Nonprofit Technology Conference (#14NTC) in order to report live and share the event in real-time with the @NonprofitOrgs community. The goals are: 1) To study how to report live effectively from a large scale, multiple day event using primarily Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook. 2) To help spread the news about the soon-to-launched .NGO and .ONG domains as well as .संगठन, .机构, & .орг. 3) To provide a summary of live event reporting best practices that other nonprofits can put into practice and benefit from.

[![Nonprofits at NTEN](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Nonprofits-at-NTEN-300x300.jpg?resize=300%2C300)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Nonprofits-at-NTEN.jpg)Through a partnership with the [Public Interest Registry](http://pir.org/), Heather Mansfield of Nonprofit Tech for Good will be attending the [Nonprofit Technology Conference](http://myntc.nten.org/home) ([#14NTC](https://twitter.com/search?q=%2314ntc)) in order to report live and share the event in real-time with the [@NonprofitOrgs](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs) community. The goals are:

1) To study how to report live effectively from a large scale, multiple day event using primarily [Twitter](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs), [Instagram](http://instagram.com/nonprofitorgs), and [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/nonprofitorgs).

2) To help spread the news about the soon-to-launched [.NGO and .ONG domains](http://globalngo.org/) as well as [.संगठन, .机构, & .орг](http://pir.org/products/chinese2char-domain/).

3) To provide a summary of live event reporting best practices that other nonprofits can put into practice and benefit from.
