Nonprofit Tech for Good is very excited to announce that the **[2019 Global NGO Technology Survey](https://funraise.org/techreport)** is now live. This year’s questions delve into web and email communications, online fundraising, social media, data management, and productivity software – and I must say, I think the design this year is awesome!

The survey is 100% anonymous and available in English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish. Please volunteer 6-8 minutes of your time and take the survey:

First released in 2016, the Global NGO Technology Survey is the world’s only crowdsourced research project for the NGO/nonprofit sector. What do I mean by crowdsourced? We’ve partnered with 24 organizations worldwide that will be promoting the survey to organizations in their own countries. Our partnership model ensures diversity in our survey respondents and expands the geographical reach of the survey.

## **[THANK YOU to our 2019 partners!](https://funraise.org/techreport-partners)**

The results of the survey will be released on September 16, 2019 in the fourth edition of the Global NGO Technology Report.

All that said, a GREAT BIG thank you as well to the new sponsor of the survey and report, **[Funraise](https://funraise.org/)**. We’re thrilled to be working together – and with our partners, to make this the best Global NGO Technology Report yet.
