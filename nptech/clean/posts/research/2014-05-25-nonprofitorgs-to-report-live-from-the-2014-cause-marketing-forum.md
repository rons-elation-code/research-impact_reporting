# @NonprofitOrgs to Report Live from the 2014 Cause Marketing Forum

> Through a partnership with the Public Interest Registry, Heather Mansfield of Nonprofit Tech for Good will be attending the Cause Marketing Forum (#CMF2014) in Chicago on May 28 and May 29 to report live and share the event in real-time with the @NonprofitOrgs community. The objectives are: 1) To share with the nonprofit community who can not be in attendance the best advice and marketing tips provided by the expert speakers at the forum. 2) To study how to report live effectively from a large scale, multiple day event using primarily Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and Google+. 3) To help spread the news about the soon-to-be launched .NGO and .ONG domains.

[![Square Graphic Annoucement Nonprofits at Cause Marketing Forum](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Square-Graphic-Annoucement-Nonprofits-at-Cause-Marketing-Forum-300x300.jpg?resize=300%2C300)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Square-Graphic-Annoucement-Nonprofits-at-Cause-Marketing-Forum.jpg)Through a partnership with the [Public Interest Registry](http://pir.org/), [Heather Mansfield](http://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/) of Nonprofit Tech for Good will be attending the [Cause Marketing Forum](http://www.causemarketingforum.com/site/c.bkLUKcOTLkK4E/b.8340961/k.4050/2014_Conference.htm) ([#CMF2014](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23CMF2014)) in Chicago on May 28 and May 29 to report live and share the event in real-time with the [@NonprofitOrgs](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs) community. The objectives are:

**1)** To share with the nonprofit community who can not be in attendance the best advice and marketing tips provided by the [expert speakers](http://www.causemarketingforum.com/site/c.bkLUKcOTLkK4E/b.8906829/k.9603/CMF2014_Program.htm) at the forum.

**2)** To study how to report live effectively from a large scale, multiple day event using primarily [Twitter](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs), [Instagram](http://instagram.com/nonprofitorgs), [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/nonprofitorgs), and [Google+](https://plus.google.com/+nonprofitorgs/posts).

**3)** To help spread the news about the soon-to-be launched [.NGO and .ONG domains](http://globalngo.org/).
