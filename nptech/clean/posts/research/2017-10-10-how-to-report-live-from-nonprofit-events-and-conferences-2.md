**The Trend of Live Reporting During Events**

- According to the [2017 Global NGO Online Technology Report](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=cdf39539dc&e=cda9a034c8), 52% of nonprofits worldwide have used social media to report live.
- Conferences, fundraising events, marches and protests, online events, TV broadcasts, and updates from the field are ideal for live reporting.
- Live reporting from nonprofit conferences and events requires an advanced skill set, training, and a lot of practice.

**Top 3 Live Reporting Tools**

- Facebook: [Share posts](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=ba8f0d94c0&e=cda9a034c8) to your Facebook Page and use [Facebook Live](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=3ae5fafd02&e=cda9a034c8) for streaming the events and interviews.
- Twitter: [Tweet in real-time](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=a1cf6efb09&e=cda9a034c8) and stream using [Periscope](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=0b4b662ee8&e=cda9a034c8).
- Instagram: [Share photos](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=61cf1dcc7b&e=cda9a034c8) and [video clips](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=28b561c9bb&e=cda9a034c8) and stream using [Live Video Stories](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=eb8f42c7d3&e=cda9a034c8).

**How Often to Post, Tweet and Stream During Live Events**

- **Facebook:** Due to the limitations of the algorithm, the best practice is to share three posts daily and use Facebook Live at least twice when live reporting. [Videos](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=4aa87a2044&e=cda9a034c8) [secure the most reach at 12.17% of total](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=d6725a1e24&e=cda9a034c8) [audience](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=17a7175bb0&e=cda9a034c8).
- **Twitter:** During live events, the ideal tweet rate is once every 8-10 minutes. Tweets can be text-based, images, graphics, or live streaming on Periscope.
- **Instagram:** Again, due to the limitations of the algorithm, share photos or videos clips three times daily and live stream a story at least twice per day.

**Before the Event: Get Organized**

- **Staff Planning:** On the day of the event your nonprofit will need at least one staff person whose sole purpose is to tweet, post, share, photograph, record, and stream.
- If you plan on conducting interviews, you’ll need two staff people – one to conduct the interviews and another to record/stream.
- **Equipment:** You’ll need a laptop and smartphone and easy access to charging stations during the event. In addition, you’ll need a [selfie stick](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=e3934fe759&e=cda9a034c8) and possibly a [tripod](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=d33d52bf1f&e=cda9a034c8).
- **Hashtags:** If your organization is hosting the event, create a short hashtag for the event ([#](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=e109ffccf5&e=cda9a034c8)[PolarPlunge](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=6349278816&e=cda9a034c8)). If it is an annual event, such as a conference, then add the year to the hashtag ([#PMDMC18](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=3a052f91ef&e=cda9a034c8)).
- Use hashtags on all pre-event promotional materials i.e., conference brochure, Facebook Invite, event landing page, social media promo graphics, etc.
- Your event hashtag should be used in every tweet, post, and graphic leading up to the event, during the event, and after the event.

**Before the Event: Content Strategy**

- Write a minimum of five call-to-action and 10 stats posts/tweets with sources in advance that can be easily copied-and-pasted during the event.
- In addition, write daily introduction tweets/posts.
- Create social media graphics for your posts/tweets.
- Create keynote and session graphics in advance.
- Create at least three quote graphics in advance.

**During the Event: How to Tweet Effectively During Live Events**

- When live reporting in real-time on Twitter, only tweet the most interesting quotes and stats presented by event speakers. The goal is not to repeat verbatim all that is being said in a sessions, but rather to share the most impressive highlights.
- At conferences, takes notes and write tweets first in Word. This helps avoid tweeting bad grammar, misspellings, and incorrect punctuation.
- During session breaks or lulls in the event, [tweet/post your pre-written tweets](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=064f071abc&e=cda9a034c8).
- [Embed](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=cf865b26d6&e=cda9a034c8) [longer quotes](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=228d94f1e9&e=cda9a034c8) on images and then share on social media.
- Retweet attendees and speakers who represent your mission and core values that are also live reporting at the event.
- If you are the host, throughout the day share links to the [event agenda](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=038baa8ec2&e=cda9a034c8) and announce new speakers by linking to their bio and headshot.

**During the Event: How to Conduct Live Interviews**

- [Conduct](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=4b6e3c1abf&e=cda9a034c8) [live video](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=2fc0bf1d7e&e=cda9a034c8) [interviews](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=47fd7f973e&e=cda9a034c8) with speakers and attendees using social media.
- Short interviews with three pre-written questions work best and share the questions with the person being interviewed in advance.
- Be cognizant of backdrops, sound, and lighting.
- In advance of the interview, verbally practice your introduction and closing statements.

**After the Event: How to Follow-up and Archive Your Event**

- At the end of event, update your event landing page with for the [next year’s event](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=eff4c3f99d&e=cda9a034c8), if applicable.
- If your conference or event has its own website, then purchase domains years in advance, such as: [bsr16.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=06eda80622&e=cda9a034c8), [bsr17.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=83d8ff5fba&e=cda9a034c8), [bsr18.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=997c6de95d&e=cda9a034c8)
- Write a blog post summarizing the event and share in an email update and on social media.
- Use [Storify](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=4101ecdaa5&e=cda9a034c8) [to archive the event](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=b98b049738&e=cda9a034c8) and/or Create a [Twitter Moment](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=6a6a50f308&e=cda9a034c8).
