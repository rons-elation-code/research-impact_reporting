# 22 Must-Read Research Reports for the Nonprofit Sector

> The reports listed below are a small sampling of some of the research available to nonprofits, NGOs, and charities worldwide. The data can be used to help your nonprofit craft your next online communication and fundraising plan. If you would like to suggest a specific report be added to the list, please contact Heather Mansfield. Most of the reports listed are based on U.S. nonprofit research. Nonprofit Tech for Good is eager to learn about, add, and promote similar reports from other coutnries/regions of the world.
