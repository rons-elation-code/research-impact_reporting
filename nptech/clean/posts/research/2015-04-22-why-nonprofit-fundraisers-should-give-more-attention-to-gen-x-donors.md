# Why Nonprofit Fundraisers Should Give More Attention to Gen X Donors

> There is a lot of discussion in the nonprofit sector about Millennials (a.k.a. Gen Y) and Baby Boomers and a growing interest in Gen Z a.k.a. #Philanthrokids, but almost no analysis of Generation X (aged 35-50 years). As the first generation to donate online and the generation that pioneered web design, email, blogging, and online advocacy, ignoring Gen X is a big mistake. As Gen X rebounds from the Great Recession, Gen Xers are entering their peak giving years and nonprofit fundraisers would be wise to pay more attention to how they give and why. 1. As the first generation to donate online and the generation that pioneered web design, email, blogging, and online advocacy, ignoring Gen X is a big mistake. As Gen X rebounds from the Great Recession, Gen Xers are entering their peak giving years and nonprofit fundraisers would be wise to pay more attention to how they give and why.

## 1. Despite the fact that Gen X lost more of its net worth during the Great Recession than other generations:

[![GEN X Recession](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GEN-X-Recession.jpg?resize=600%2C437)](http://www.businessinsider.com/actually-its-generation-x-that-is-suffering-most-in-this-recession-2012-6)**Source: [Business Insider](http://www.businessinsider.com/actually-its-generation-x-that-is-suffering-most-in-this-recession-2012-6)**

## 2. Gen X gives generously to nonprofits – an average of $1,033.28 annually.

[![Gen X Gives](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Gen-X-Gives.png?resize=600%2C146)](http://www.abila.com/lpgs/donorengagementstudy/)**Source: [Abila](http://www.abila.com/lpgs/donorengagementstudy/)**

## 3. Gen X volunteers more than other generations. In fact, 29.7% of Gen Xers in the United States volunteer.

[![Gen-X-Volunteers](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Gen-X-Volunteers1.jpg?resize=600%2C477)](http://www.volunteeringinamerica.gov/special/Generation-X)**Source: [Corporation for National and Community Service](http://www.volunteeringinamerica.gov/special/Generation-X)**

## 4. Thus, nonprofits should give special attention to their Gen X volunteers since volunteers give twice as much as non-volunteers.

[![Volunteers Give More](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Volunteers-Give-More.png?resize=600%2C522)](http://www.volunteeringinamerica.gov/infographic.cfm)**Source: [Corporation for National and Community Service](http://www.volunteeringinamerica.gov/special/Generation-X)**
