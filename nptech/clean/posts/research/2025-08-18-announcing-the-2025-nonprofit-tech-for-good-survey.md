# The Results of 2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Survey Will Be Released March 2026 ✅

> The 2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Survey consists of 20 easy-to-answer questions about website and email marketing, online fundraising, social media marketing, and artificial intelligence. Depending upon your answers, the survey only requires 3-7 minutes to complete!

The 2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Survey is now closed! 826 nonprofits participated and the results will be published in March 2026 in the second edition of our Nonprofit Tech for Good Report.

63% of the participants are small nonprofits, 31% are medium-sized, and 6% are large. 66% are based is the United States, 10% in Canada, and 9% in Europe.

To be alerted of the report’s release, please sign up for [Nonprofit Tech for Good’s newsletter](https://subscribepage.io/HOjlsE). 📨
