![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/media-adcisory-tech-report-ngos-300x300.jpg?resize=300%2C300)The inaugural edition of the [**2017 Global Trends in Giving Report**](https://www.funraise.org/giving-report/past-reports) will be released on September 12, 2017. Based on the survey responses of 4,084 donors worldwide, the report summarizes donor data across six continents about how online and mobile technology effects giving. The report also examines the impact of gender, generation, and ideology upon giving and volunteerism.

Researched by Nonprofit Tech for Good, the report is an annual research project that seeks to gain a better understanding of how donors worldwide prefer to give and engage with their favorite causes and charitable organizations. The report will be released in English, French and Spanish.

If you are blogger or journalist and on September 7 would like to receive a press release and early embargoed release of the report and corresponding infographics, please email Heather Mansfield at **[heather@nptechforgood.com](mailto:heather@nptechforgood.com)**. Please include your name, the name of your blog or publication, and the country in which you are based. Thank you.
