# 1. Nearly one in three (31.5%) people worldwide donated to charity in 2015 and one in four (24%) volunteered.

[![PIR-Illustrations](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/NGOfact-2-FB-SMALL.jpg?resize=600%2C600)](http://techreport.ngo/facts-and-stats-about-ngos-worldwide.html)

**Source: [CAF World Giving Index 2015](http://techreport.ngo/facts-and-stats-about-ngos-worldwide.html)**

# 2. By 2018, the global population of social media users is projected to grow to 2.44 billion, or 33% of the world population.

[![global social media audience](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/global-social-media-audience.png?resize=600%2C281)](http://trackmaven.com/blog/2016/03/social-media-trends/)

**Source: [TrackMaven](http://trackmaven.com/blog/2016/03/social-media-trends/)**

# 3. Total giving is expected to grow 4.1% in 2016 and 4.3% in 2017. The vast majority of the giving will be done by individuals.

[![total giving growth](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/total-giving-growth.png?resize=600%2C406)](http://162.211.248.56/index.php/philanthropy-outlook/total-giving/)

**Source: [The Philanthropy Outlook](http://162.211.248.56/index.php/philanthropy-outlook/total-giving/)**

**Source: [Abila](http://www.abila.com/lpgs/donorloyaltystudy/)**
