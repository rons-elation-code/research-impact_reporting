## 

The 2020 Global Trends in Giving Survey closed June 1 and the full results will be released September 14 in the third edition of the [**Global Trends in Giving Report**](http://www.nptechforgood.com/research/). 7,008 survey respondents from 81 countries indicated in the survey that they had donated in response to the COVID-19 pandemic and some characteristics of these donors are listed below.

It’s worth noting that 68% of the COVID-19 donors were female, 31% male. 41% were Baby Boomers, 24% Millennials, 22% Gen X, 11% Matures, and 2% Gen Z.

---

## 1) The top three preferred methods of giving are online (80.5%), bank/wire transfer (18.6%), and direct mail/post (14.4%).

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Preferred-Method-of-Donating.jpg?resize=833%2C819)

## 2) 53.6% of COVID-19 donors are enrolled in a recurring giving program.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Monthly-Donors-to-Nonprofits.jpg?resize=829%2C684)

## 3) 49.7% have donated to a crowdfunding campaign benefiting a nonprofit within the last 12 months.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Crowdfunding-for-Nonprofits.jpg?resize=828%2C567)

## 4) 12.3% have created a peer-to-peer fundraising campaign benefiting a nonprofit within the last 12 months.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Peer-to-Peer-Fundraising-COVID-Donors.jpg?resize=826%2C569)

## 5) 20.9% of COVID-19 donors also donated to a nonprofit on #GivingTuesday 2019.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/GIvingTuesday-2019-COVID.jpg?resize=824%2C583)

## 6) 32.6% give to nonprofits located outside of their country of residence. The top three countries are the U.S. (24.9%), Canada (9.7%), and India (4.2%).

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Give-outside-country-of-residence-COVID-donors.jpg?resize=828%2C528)

## 7) 66.7% have volunteered for a nonprofit within the last 12 months. 74% donated to the nonprofit that they volunteered for.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Volunteer-COVID-donors.jpg?resize=830%2C529)

## 8) 32% of COVID-19 donors have donated through Facebook Fundraising Tools. Of those, 88% said they are likely to do it again.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Facebook-Fundraising-COVID-Donors.jpg?resize=827%2C644)

## 9) 10.7% have donated through Instagram Fundraising Tools. Of those, 93% said they are likely to do it again.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/Instagram-Fundraising-Tools-COVID-donors.jpg?resize=828%2C644)

## 10) 64.8% of COVID-19 donors say that regular email communication about the impact of their donation is most likely to inspire them to give repeatedly to an organization.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/How-to-Thank-COVID-19-Donors.jpg?resize=829%2C660)
