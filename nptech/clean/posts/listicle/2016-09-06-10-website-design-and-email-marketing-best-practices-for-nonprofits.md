## **10 Website Design Best Practices for NGOs**

**1.** For your website and blog to be compatible with desktops, laptops, smartphones, smart TVs, etc., your NGO must **[mobile-optimize your content](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=be06d07462&e=cda9a034c8)**. WordPress.org is a **[content management system for websites and blogs](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=798bd8f9aa&e=cda9a034c8)**. There are **[hundreds of responsively designed, low cost WordPress.org templates for NGOs](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=88634ac4fc&e=cda9a034c8)** and **[freelancers available for hire](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=75fbb403b3&e=cda9a034c8)**.

**2.** Prioritize images over text and provide ample white space: **[pbs.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=7e162d4d00&e=cda9a034c8) ([on January 5, 2015](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=d06a930833&e=cda9a034c8))**

**3.** Use a large font size for title and body content: **[npca.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=53a408e309&e=cda9a034c8)**

**4.** Provide simple navigation and allow long scrolling: **[worldwildlife.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=9ddb601502&e=cda9a034c8)**

**5.** Prominently feature your “Donate” button, social network icons, and e-newsletter opt-in: **[themarshallproject.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=436fc8947a&e=cda9a034c8)**

**6.** Use large tappable buttons for calls-to-action: **[water.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=835dce76d0&e=cda9a034c8)**

**7.** Maximize your website and blog’s right bar: **[aspca.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=02b259f9da&e=cda9a034c8)**

**8.**Host your blog/stories\* inside your website: **[audubon.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=c357550cd7&e=cda9a034c8)**

**9.**Use the **[Facebook Comments Plugin](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=94ffbfcb1c&e=cda9a034c8)** or **[Disqus](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=185cf00e0c&e=cda9a034c8)** for blog comments.

**10.**Ensure that your website and blog is shareable: **[oceana.org](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=377a992acf&e=cda9a034c8)**

## **10 Email Marketing Best Practices for NGOs**

**1.** Never send email newsletters BCC or as a PDF attachment. Your NGO must invest in an **[email marketing service](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=9d8a9187a0&e=cda9a034c8)**.

**2.**Embrace a mobile-first email strategy. Mobile-optimize your email **([UNICEF on desktop](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=741b93ad93&e=cda9a034c8), [UNICEF on smartphone](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=10290e5aa5&e=cda9a034c8))** by using a **[responsive template](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=70d64a30ce&e=cda9a034c8)**.

**3.** Design templates that allow **[header images](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=82539b0bd1&e=cda9a034c8)** to be easily changed.

**4.** Integrate social media into your templates by featuring **[social network icons](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=1461022ab6&e=cda9a034c8)** (and a “Donate” button) into your templates and by adding the ability for subscribers to **[share stories](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=98729e8b35&e=cda9a034c8)**, **[pin products](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=bd524c0270&e=cda9a034c8)**, **[like your Instagram photos](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=933d55f867&e=cda9a034c8)**, etc.

**5.** Focus on **[1-5 stories and/or calls-to action](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=8c217cc6d6&e=cda9a034c8)** and keep text to a minimum.

**6.** Prioritize **[image](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=8c15e42075&e=cda9a034c8)** and **[video](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=037f31ea83&e=cda9a034c8)** content.

**7.** Write **[short subject lines](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=2524ede447&e=cda9a034c8)** (50 characters or less) and use **[emojis](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=a5315a016a&e=cda9a034c8)** to maximize open rates.

**8.**Send e-newsletters **[twice per month](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=5a3f532c45&e=cda9a034c8)** and donation appeals an average of once per month with **[increased frequency in December](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=5fef77f2cb&e=cda9a034c8)**.

**9.** Study the email marketing of large NGOs similar to yours in mission and programs by making an online donation and/or signing up for their e-newsletter.

**10. [Prioritize growing your email list](http://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=598e1835ce&e=cda9a034c8)** in 2016 and 2017.
