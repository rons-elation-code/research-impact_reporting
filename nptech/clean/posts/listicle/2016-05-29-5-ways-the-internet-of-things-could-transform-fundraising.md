# 1) Household Appliances

[![5-Samsung-Family-Hub-Refrigerator-Announced-in-CES-2016-with-Large-Display-1](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/5-Samsung-Family-Hub-Refrigerator-Announced-in-CES-2016-with-Large-Display-1.jpg?resize=600%2C320)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/5-Samsung-Family-Hub-Refrigerator-Announced-in-CES-2016-with-Large-Display-1.jpg)

## ***Imagine:***A breaking news story flashes on your refrigerator screen with an option to finger scan an [urgent donation to a community in need](http://www.wfp.org/emergencies/syria).

# 2) Automobiles

[![Tesla-Apps-P90D-Touchscreen](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/Tesla-Apps-P90D-Touchscreen.jpg?resize=600%2C320)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/Tesla-Apps-P90D-Touchscreen.jpg)

## *Imagine:* Listening to your local public media during a pledge drive and [signing up to become a monthly sustainer](http://www.npr.org/stations/) through voice recognition while driving.

# 3) Smart TVs

[![SmartPay_TV_Main_1](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/SmartPay_TV_Main_1.jpg?resize=600%2C320)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/SmartPay_TV_Main_1.jpg)

## *Imagine:* Watching an evening news segment about the poaching crisis in Africa and pointing your remote (or smartphone) at the screen to easily [donate to an anti-poaching unit](http://www.blackmambas.org/).

# 4) Public Space Advertising

[![maxresdefault](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/maxresdefault.jpg?resize=600%2C320)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/maxresdefault.jpg)

## *Imagine:* Waiting for your morning bus and being able to [add your name to a petition or donate to an awareness campaign](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fiJsV54m9xI) through [near field communication](http://nfc-forum.org/what-is-nfc/) on an ad screen at the bus stop (or subway, airport, etc.).

# 5) Smartwatches

[![apple-watch-left-hand](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/apple-watch-left-hand.png?resize=600%2C320)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/apple-watch-left-hand.png)

## *Imagine*: [Checking your Twitter](https://support.twitter.com/articles/20172678) on your smartwatch while on a walk and being able to easily donate to your favorite nonprofit on Twitter by simply tapping a [$Cashtag](https://cash.me/cashtags) in a tweet.
