# 10 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Trends to Prioritize in 2023

> From social media being overrated to Instagram Fundraising Tools to online privacy laws and cybersecurity, this post features the top ten digital marketing and fundraising trends for nonprofits to prioritize in 2023.

Each year [Heather Mansfield of Nonprofit Tech for Good](https://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/) presents a free webinar featuring the top ten digital marketing and fundraising trends to prioritize in the following year. This year’s webinar, [10 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Trends to Prioritize in 2023](https://register.gotowebinar.com/recording/9181763645956470955), was presented on December 7. The notes from this year’s webinar are featured below and you can [access the recording](https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/recording/9181763645956470955).

## **1) Know that the effectiveness of social media is often overrated.**

The nonprofit sector needs to have an honest conversation about the effectiveness of social media. Too often, social media companies release new data and recommendations that are meant to inspire nonprofits to use social media more often, but increasingly, the data is biased and the recommendations are more beneficial to the social media companies than to nonprofits.

As a sector, we’ve been addicted to the buzz about social media – and social media itself – for nearly 20 years, but if we take a step back and take a critical look at the big picture of social media, many nonprofits will discover that their return on investment (ROI) from using social media is minimal.

**First,** organic reach is at an all-time low and the [value and effectiveness of social media advertising is questionable](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=542f68a038&e=cda9a034c8):

- **Facebook:** 2.9 billion monthly active users – organic reach is estimated to be 4%.
- **Instagram:** 1.4 billion monthly active users – organic reach is estimated to be 6%.
- **LinkedIn:** 310 million monthly active users – organic reach is estimated to be 4%.
- **Twitter:** 238 million monthly active users – organic reach is estimated to be 4%.

**Second,** and as a result of record-low organic reach, referral traffic from social media is also at a record low.

**Third,** social media companies are in chaos at the moment and 2023 is likely to be a rough year. Meta’s advertising revenue model is failing and their future is unpredictable. Elon Musk’s Twitter is chaotic, and if it continues on its current path, many nonprofits will be forced to pause or leave Twitter. Also, research continues to reveal that [social media is dangerous to mental health](https://counterhate.com/research/deadly-by-design/) and that [disinformation on social media is eroding democracy](https://disinfo2022.com/). As a whole, there is not much positive to say about the social media companies these days.

**Thus,** have an honest look at your social media campaigns:

1. *How is your referral traffic from social media?* If you are receiving minimal website traffic from social media, it’s time to either invest in social media ads or post less often.
2. *What is your engagement rate?* Like organic reach, engagement rates are at an all-time low.
3. *Are you raising funds using [Facebook & Instagram Fundraising Tools](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=f67111f2d9&e=cda9a034c8)?* If the tools are performing for your nonprofit, then keep at it in 2023.

If you discover that your return on investment (ROI) from social media is low, then **2023 is a great year to shift your priorities to other digital marketing and fundraising tools that have been neglected due to an overfocus on social media.**

**What about TikTok, WhatsApp, and Mastodon?**

**TikTok:** There is a lot of pressure right now to create (time-consuming) long-form videos for TikTok (and Instagram Reels), but only experiment if you have 3-10 hours per week and staff that are comfortable on camera. Currently, [the average engagement rate for nonprofits on TikTok is 7.6%](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=4b99754b90&e=cda9a034c8) and [1.95% for Instagram Reels](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=86b406c9d3&e=cda9a034c8). As more nonprofits and brands join TikTok, reach will likely drop.

**WhatsApp:** With Meta struggling due to its hyperfocus on the Metaverse over the last 12 months, Meta plans to expand the functionality of [WhatsApp](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=43f7e6dbe6&e=cda9a034c8). For nonprofits, [WhatsApp Communities](https://blog.whatsapp.com/communities-now-available) are the tool to watch.

**Mastodon:** [A Twitter alternative](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=c00bf265c0&e=cda9a034c8), but even with all the chaos of the Elon Musk takeover, any Twitter alternative will struggle. As of now, Mastodon seems to be made up of users mostly from the liberal/progressive spectrum while Truth Social is for the conservative/right wing of the political spectrum. Siloed social media is problematic and frankly, boring. A viable Twitter alternative will need to attract a wide variety of individuals and brands that can co-exist online with minimal content moderation.

## **2) Prioritize the core tools of digital marketing and fundraising in 2023.**

If your nonprofit decides to invest less time in social media in 2023, then use that extra time to complete items on your to do list that have been overlooked or neglected. The recommendations listed below can be implemented quickly and positively impact your nonprofit’s digital marketing and fundraising strategy:

**1. Website**

- Give your website a quick refresh with new photos, feature stories, and an updated [right bar](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=498b2b2cc5&e=cda9a034c8). Most right bars on nonprofit websites are left blank, but click-through rates are often higher than links in navigation bars.
- Apply and utilize [Google and Microsoft Ads Grants](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=d3b4edd511&e=cda9a034c8).
- Prioritize SEO by publishing content on a regular basis to your website, such as a “News” section or [blog](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=e37c0df83e&e=cda9a034c8). Search engines are constantly crawling, indexing, and ranking websites. Nonprofit Tech for Good is offering a free webinar on March 29, [Blogging Best Practices for Nonprofits](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/free-webinar-blogging-best-practices-for-nonprofits/).

**2. Email Marketing & Fundraising**

- 68% of nonprofits send email campaigns once per month or less, thus send email campaigns more often! Email is a top driver of online donations, RSVPs to events, and traffic to your website. The value of sending email more often, in most cases, outweighs the risk of alienating your subscribers.
- [According to the Nonprofit Communications Trends Report](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=41142132a2&e=cda9a034c8), 69% of nonprofits say they never stop emailing even if the subscriber is no longer engaged. Delete unengaged subscribers!
- Check to see if your domain is on an [email server blacklist](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=812a6fa26c&e=cda9a034c8).

**3. Donation Pages**

- Donate to your own nonprofit to confirm the [thank you landing page and follow-up email](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=af8c2da31f&e=cda9a034c8) are current and designed to feature important calls-to-action (CTAs).
- Consider adding [wallet payments](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=6c74077a7b&e=cda9a034c8), [Donate](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=626f510fbd&e=cda9a034c8) [Now, Pay Later](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=0319b6f4c7&e=cda9a034c8), and ACH to your donation pages.
- Create and promote a [major donor donation page/program](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=dc0239b79b&e=cda9a034c8).

## **3) Implement a donor retention strategy.**

While not as fun as using social media, implementing a donor retention strategy is likely to result in more revenue and dedicated supporters.

- According to the [Fundraising Effectiveness Report](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=51c91a10c2&e=cda9a034c8), the overall donor retention rate for 2021 was 43.6% — meaning that 56.4% of donors that gave in 2021 are likely not to give in 2022. It can be 5x more cost-effective to retain donors than recruit new ones.
- Always [thank your donors](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=002a6f716f&e=cda9a034c8) after each donation via email and create a new donor welcome email series. [ThankView](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=019152fd20&e=cda9a034c8) allows nonprofits to easily send personalized “Thank You” videos to donors.
- Publish quarterly or bi-annual [Impact reports](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=7a28ab3af3&e=cda9a034c8) in addition to your annual report. ([55+ Templates](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=dd360dce4c&e=cda9a034c8) and [Canva](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=cf53cec0a5&e=cda9a034c8))
- Send a donation summary at the end of the year for tax purposes.

## **4) Invest in professional graphic design.**

The rise in social media has resulted in the expectation that digital marketing and fundraising professionals be skilled in graphic design, but the ability to effectively use Canva isn’t enough. Some projects absolutely require hiring professional graphic designers.

- [Canva for Nonprofits](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=38d08c2b60&e=cda9a034c8) is an excellent graphic design resource for nonprofits, but for special fundraising and awareness campaigns, nonprofits need a budget to hire professional graphic designers.
- [Fiverr](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=d773d4815e&e=cda9a034c8) and [Upwork](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=1e2a27bae7&e=cda9a034c8) are online marketplaces of freelancers. You can hire individuals to create [social media posts & banners](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=67086d261d&e=cda9a034c8), [Infographics](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=1e66cc8568&e=cda9a034c8), [Email templates](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=b35ab31ae8&e=cda9a034c8), [Website banners](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=d561ea4227&e=cda9a034c8)

## **5) Launch a tribute giving program.**

As the saying goes, what’s old is new again. Nonprofits began to offer tribute gifts in the early 2000s and then the concept fell off the radar screen of most nonprofits in the 2010s. Today, the technology available makes launching or re-launching a new-and-improved tribute giving program an obvious fit for the 2020s.

- [Global Trends in Giving Report](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=ccdb105259&e=cda9a034c8): 33% of donors give tribute gifts to family and friends.
- Donors are most interested in giving tribute gifts as memorials (43%), birthday gifts (25%), and on religious holidays (10%), such as Christmas, Hanukkah, and [Eid al-Fitr](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=44bfeaf8ea&e=cda9a034c8).
- Add a tribute giving option to your [primary donation page](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=e11497f2c7&e=cda9a034c8). [[Give WP](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=b3e55c2afb&e=cda9a034c8)]
- [Create a tribute giving landing page](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=6d96248b57&e=cda9a034c8) and add it to your [Ways to Give page](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=98191f89bd&e=cda9a034c8).
- To promote your tribute giving program in print and online, create a redirect: [redcross.org/tribute-giving](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=6d40ecbcbb&e=cda9a034c8)
- Download [How to Launch & Grow a Tribute Giving Program](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=449910b103&e=cda9a034c8).

## **6) Embrace Instagram Fundraising Tools.**

The power of Facebook Fundraising Tools seems to have plateaued over the last 12 months, but the power of Instagram Fundraising Tools have yet to be realized. 2023 is a good year to start experimenting with fundraising on Instagram if you haven’t already.

- According to the [Global Trends in Giving Report](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=056d744e9c&e=cda9a034c8), 10% of online donors have donated through [Instagram Fundraising Tools](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=142514389a&e=cda9a034c8). Of those, 93% said they are likely to give through Instagram again.
- [Confirm the tools are available in your country](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=89a10006fb&e=cda9a034c8), then connect your nonprofit’s Instagram Business Account to your nonprofit’s verified Facebook Page.
- Your nonprofit must be approved to use Facebook Fundraising Tools. Start with [Meta Social Impact on Instagram](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=0dd17057b4&e=cda9a034c8).
- What the tools offer: 1) “Support” buttons, 2) [Nonprofit Fundraisers](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=e9b66e12b3&e=cda9a034c8) in posts, reels, and live videos 3) Donation stickers in stories
- [Ask your supporters to create Instagram Fundraisers](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=6c743bbaaf&e=cda9a034c8) on your website and on Instagram.

## **8) Consider launching a texting campaign.**

Text messaging is on the verge of a comeback. Nonprofits embraced text messaging and text-to-give in the years after the [2010 earthquake in Haiti](https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/HaitiEarthquake/haiti-earthquake-donations-haiti-relief-efforts-text-message/story?id=9551199), but since then, the technology has improved in its efficiency and cost.

- [Text messages have a 98% open rate](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=06cd520f53&e=cda9a034c8), compared to 26% for email, and 4-6% organic reach for most social media.
- [Ask donors for their phone number](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=554963eaf9&e=cda9a034c8), but do not require it on donation forms ([TCPA](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=dc70118d75&e=cda9a034c8)).
- [Nonprofits can use text](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=87ecf2d60a&e=cda9a034c8) for urgent fundraising appeals and calls-to-action, but also for relationship building: 1) News updates 2) Behind-the-scenes photos ([MMS](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=2dc7788430&e=cda9a034c8)) 3) Thank you messages 4) Voice memos.

## **9) Stay current on online privacy law.**

New online privacy legislation is being enacted all over the world. The law is finally starting to catch up with the internet and privacy rights are a legal trend that nonprofits cannot afford to ignore.

- On January 1, 2023, the [California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (CCPA)](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=8137256185&e=cda9a034c8) will extend more privacy rights to California residents.
- Privacy policies are also required by the existing regulations [SHIELD in New York](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=1b7ea656d2&e=cda9a034c8) and [GDPR in Europe](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=02825ca68b&e=cda9a034c8). [[Check Your Compliance](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=9ae6f9d689&e=cda9a034c8)]
- Important: [Pre-ticked check boxes](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=45af7c2bfd&e=cda9a034c8) are no longer legal under GDPR and CASL in Canada – that is a trend likely to come to the United States in the coming years.

## **10) Prioritize cybersecurity.**

According to the [2023 Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=3896d7ad14&e=cda9a034c8), 27% of nonprofits worldwide have experienced a cyberattack (email phishing, website hacking, ransomware, social media attack, etc.). Cybersecurity cannot be ignored in 2023.

- Use a [premium hosting service](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=11ce6f0675&e=cda9a034c8) for your website that prioritizes speed, security, and automated daily back-ups.
- Require a [double opt-in and captcha](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=1dc18a57ac&e=cda9a034c8) to subscribe to your email lists.
- Enable [two-factor](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=66f9b86f9b&e=cda9a034c8)[authentification](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=32c183ba4e&e=cda9a034c8) for all social media accounts.
- Use a [password management tool](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=535e9d332a&e=cda9a034c8).
- Update your software often, [back up your data daily](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=182c16d645&e=cda9a034c8), secure your WiFi networks, require strong passwords, install [anti-virus software](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=bb96bb9839&e=cda9a034c8), and require authentication.
- [Create a Cybersecurity Plan](https://nptechforgood.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=e9d227595303f56fb14c8ddb7&id=4c9b475712&e=cda9a034c8) and share it with employees.
