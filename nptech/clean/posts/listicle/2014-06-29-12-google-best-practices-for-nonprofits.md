[![12 Google+ Page Best Practices for Nonprofits](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/12-Google%2B-Page-Best-Practices-for-Nonprofits-300x300.jpg?resize=300%2C300)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/12-Google%2B-Page-Best-Practices-for-Nonprofits.jpg)**[Google+ Pages](https://plus.google.com/+nonprofitorgs/posts)** have a vast tool set and Google’s quest to integrate Google+ into their most popular products, such as Search, Gmail, YouTube, Local, and Wallet, is paying off as **[active monthly users continue to grow](http://www.pinterest.com/pin/203154633166200962/)**. That said, Google+ is low on the priority lists of most nonprofits. Many nonprofits are curious, but lack the resources necessary to create and manage yet another social network.

Google+ isn’t for all nonprofits, but for those that have actively adopted a pro-social media policy, it’s worth the time investment to experiment with Google+. In the process, your nonprofit at the very least will increase your **[SEO in Google Search](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2013/04/29/google-search-results-now-prominently-feature-nonprofit-google-pages/)** and position yourself as an early adopter of **[digital wallets](http://www.nten.org/articles/2014/mobile-for-good-how-mobile-wallets-will-transform-fundraising)**. For those nonprofits already active on Google+ or for those willing to take the leap, below are 12 Google+ Page best practices to help get you started.

---

## 1. Prioritize uploading photos over sharing links.

Google+ is a visual social network. Posts with with a message and an uploaded image get much higher engagement than shared links.

[![Jane Goodall Google+](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jane-Goodall-Google%2B.png?resize=600%2C565)](https://plus.google.com/103858765220369650172/posts/F8gmSNh6Pfo)

1. Write your post and include a link.  
2. Delete the generated thumbnail and link that appears.  
3. Upload a larger version the photo and then share.

## 2. Experiment with animated .GIFs.

What’s old is new again. Animated .GIFs are making a comeback. You can upload them as posts or as cover photos on Google+ Pages. See the **[Sierra Club’s Google+ Page](https://plus.google.com/+sierraclub/posts)** to view an example of an animated .GIF being used as a cover photo.

[![Sierra Club an Google+](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Sierra-Club-an-Google%2B.png?resize=600%2C271)](https://plus.google.com/+sierraclub/posts)

## 3. Curate good content through sharing.

Most nonprofits do not create enough of their own content to be active daily on Google+. By sharing other’s content, you can effectively engage and inspire your followers.

[![Global Citizen an Google+ 3](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Global-Citizen-an-Google%2B-3.png?resize=600%2C318)](https://plus.google.com/+GLBLCTZN/posts/8VgwHoXHzkk)

[![Global Citizen an Google+ 2](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Global-Citizen-an-Google%2B-2.png?resize=600%2C454)](https://plus.google.com/+GLBLCTZN/posts/8VgwHoXHzkk)

## 4. Tag other pages in your poststo foster good will.

By adding a “+” symbol in front of page names, your nonprofit can foster good will with funders, partners, and supporters.

[![Public Interest Registry on Google+](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Public-Interest-Registry-on-Google%2B.png?resize=600%2C302)](https://plus.google.com/+nonprofitorgs/posts)

## 5. +1 the content posted by others to increase the exposure of your nonprofit’s avatar.

Regularly “+1” the content of other to increase exposure of your nonprofit’s Google+ Page and thus grow your followers.

[![WWF on Google+](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/WWF-on-Google%2B.png?resize=600%2C475)](https://plus.google.com/b/103858765220369650172/+WWF/posts)

## 6. Merge your YouTube Channel with your nonprofit’s Google+ Page.

Google is committed to weaving Google+ through out their suite of products and **[merging your nonprofit’s YouTube Channel with your Google+ Page](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2013/05/05/how-to-merge-your-nonprofits-youtube-channel-and-google-page/)** can help increase both your Google Search SEO and your TouTube SEO.

[![Amnesty International Google+](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Amnesty-International-Google%2B1.png?resize=600%2C415)](https://plus.google.com/+amnestyusa/posts)

## 7. Have more than one manager for your Google+ Page.

In your Dashboard under “Settings” add at least two managers to ensure that your nonprofit does not lose access to your Google+ Page due to staff and volunteer turnover.

[![Google+ Managers](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-Managers1.png?resize=600%2C327)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-Managers1.png)

## 8. Reserve your Google+ Page URL.

In your Dashboard under “About” reserve a Google+ Page URL for your nonprofit. Ideally your URLs are consistent across all of your nonprofit’s social networks.

[![Google+ URL](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-URL.png?resize=600%2C307)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-URL.png)

## 9. Regularly monitor your Google+ Page Insights.

Google+ Insights will help your nonprofit get a clear understanding of what kind of content your followers engage with most as well as their demographics.

[![Insights 1](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Insights-1.png?resize=600%2C302)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Insights-1.png)

[![Insights 2](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Insights-2.png?resize=600%2C236)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Insights-2.png)

[![Insights 3](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Insights-3.png?resize=600%2C268)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Insights-3.png)

## 10. Create “Events” for Google+ Hangouts on Air.

**[Google+ Events](http://www.google.com/+/learnmore/events/)** allow followers to RSVP to your Hangout on Air and enable real-time conversations while the Hangout on Air is occurring. Events also serve as a chronological history of your nonprofit’s Google+ Hangouts on Air.

[![Google+ Events 1](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-Events-1.png?resize=600%2C298)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-Events-1.png)[![Google+ Events 2](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-Events-2.png?resize=600%2C520)](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google%2B-Events-2.png)

## 11. Claim your Google+ Local Page.

Location-based nonprofits already have a presence on Google+ though most do not know it. **[Google+ Local Pages](https://plus.google.com/108601826331581658642/about)** are given priority placement in Google Search results, so be sure to sign up for [**Google+ Local**](http://www.google.com/+/learnmore/local/) and claim your Google+ Local Page.

[![MOMA on Google+](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/MOMA-on-Google%2B1.png?resize=600%2C458)](https://plus.google.com/108601826331581658642/about)

## **12. Sign up for the Google for Nonprofits Program.**

When (and let’s hope it is soon) Google+ adds Google Wallet “Donate” buttons to Google+ Pages **[(like it has to YouTube Channels)](https://www.youtube.com/user/SavetheChildrenUSA)**, your nonprofit will be one step ahead in utilizing digital wallets for online fundraising. The first step is to sign up for the **[Google for Nonprofits Program](http://www.google.com/nonprofits/)**.

[![Google for Nonprofits](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Google-for-Nonprofits1.png?resize=600%2C318)](http://www.google.com/nonprofits/)

[![nonprofit google donate wallet button](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/nonprofit-google-donate-wallet-button.png?resize=600%2C310)](https://www.youtube.com/user/SavetheChildrenUSA)
