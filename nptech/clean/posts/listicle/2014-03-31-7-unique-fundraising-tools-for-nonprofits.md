## 1. Face the Planet [facetheplanet.com](http://www.facetheplanet.com/)

Face the Planet enables users to hypothetically purchase real estate ($3-4 per landmark) and 85% of the proceeds are donated to environmental and wildlife conservation organizations. Watch this one! More gaming, geolocation, and social functionality to be added soon. **And if your nonprofit is working to conserve the planet and its wild inhabitants,** [contact the founder directly](http://www.facetheplanet.com/aboutus) to see about having your nonprofit listed.

[![Face the Planet](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Face-the-Planet.png?resize=600%2C342)](http://www.facetheplanet.com/)

**Purchase properties and select the nonprofit to benefit:**

[![Face the Planet 2](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Face-the-Planet-2.png?resize=600%2C395)](http://www.facetheplanet.com/)

**You are then attached to the property and added to a map:**

[![tower grove park](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/tower-grove-park.png?resize=600%2C346)](http://www.facetheplanet.com/map)

**And you earn badges and property certificates:**

[![badges](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/badges.png?resize=600%2C210)](http://www.facetheplanet.com/map)

[![my properties](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/my-properties.png?resize=600%2C240)](http://www.facetheplanet.com/map)

## 2. Wizeo [wizeo.org](https://www.wizeo.org)

Wizeo is an online platform where members can bid to have one-on-one 15-minute video chats or donate to attend a group video chat with participating thought leaders and celebrities.

[![Wizeo](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Wizeo.png?resize=600%2C346)](https://www.wizeo.org)

## 3. Guilty Pledgers [guiltypledgers.com](https://guiltypledgers.com)

Guilty Pledgers is a mobile app launched by [Spotify](https://www.spotify.com) that enables users to create fundraising parties where guests can pledge money to add their favorite songs to the party playlist.

[![guilty pledgers](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/guilty-pledgers.png?resize=600%2C283)](https://guiltypledgers.com)

## 4. Ink to the People [inktothepeople.com](http://inktothepeople.com/)

Ink to the People empowers anyone to create and sell their own t-shirt designs to benefit their favorite nonprofits.

[![tshirt fundraiser](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/tshirt-fundraiser.png?resize=600%2C311)](http://inktothepeople.com/)

## 5. Fundography [fundography.com](http://www.fundography.com/)

Fundography allows event attendees to upload and share photos taken at the event and then event attendees can purchase the photos for $2 each to benefit their favorite nonprofit.

[![fundography](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fundography.png?resize=600%2C316)](http://www.fundography.com/)

## 6. GiveMob [givemobapp.org](http://www.givemobapp.org/)

GiveMob is a mobile app that allows users to donate to select nonprofits via text with just a couple of taps.

[![GiveMob](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/GiveMob.png?resize=600%2C342)](http://www.givemobapp.org/)

## **7. Cause Direct [causepayments.com](http://www.causepayments.com)**

Cause Direct enables businesses to donate to their favorite charity or school every time they accept a credit or debit card payment.

[![Cause Direct](https://i0.wp.com/www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Cause-Direct.png?resize=600%2C312)](http://www.causepayments.com/)
