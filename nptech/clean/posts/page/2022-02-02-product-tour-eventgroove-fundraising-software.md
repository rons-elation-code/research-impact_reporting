In this 30-minute product tour of Eventgroove, learn how your nonprofit can easily host online, in-person, and hybrid fundraisers, sell tickets for raffles and auctions, and accept donations online through your website.

Built to help your nonprofit fundraise and run events, [Eventgroove is an all-in-one integrated fundraising platform](https://www.eventgroove.com/) that embraces a customer-first approach and provides superb support from its Montana-based team.

[This product demo](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/5624533461010095118) will provide an overview of Eventgroove and its capabilities, including:

- Branded end-to-end fundraising campaigns – from donation pages to crowdfunding
- Online raffles, auctions, A-thons, sweepstakes, and sales fundraisers
- Integrated live streaming
- Team fundraising a.k.a. peer-to-peer fundraising
- Effortless (and contactless) event check-in
- At-your-fingertips donor analytics and engagement
- Print-on-demand and e-commerce products branded to your nonprofit
- Promotional tools, including social sharing, automated email campaigns, and text-to-give
- Integrations with Mailchimp, Facebook, Constant Contact, Twilio, Blackbaud, and more
