# [Recordings] Earn a 2024 Certificate in Social Media Marketing & Fundraising

> Customized for nonprofits with limited time and financial resources, the certificate program covers the fundamentals of social media marketing and fundraising for your nonprofit. Participants will learn how to create a social media strategy, craft a content marketing plan, and current best practices for using social media for community engagement and fundraising.

Customized for nonprofits with limited time and financial resources, the [2024 Certificate in Social Media Marketing & Fundraising program](https://buy.stripe.com/3csg2u2oZ75Tfy8aEZ) covers the fundamentals of social media marketing and fundraising for your nonprofit.

With a focus on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and Threads for nonprofits, participants will learn how to create a social media strategy, craft a content marketing plan, and current best practices for using social media for community engagement and fundraising. To earn the certificate, the following three webinar recordings are required:

**1) Social Media Strategy for Nonprofits  
Presented July 10, 2024 • 2 hours**

- A 5-step template to write a social media strategy for your nonprofit
- How to track and report the success of your social media strategy
- How to create a consistent brand across social media platforms
- How to grow your social media following

**2) Social Media Content Marketing for Nonprofits  
Presented July 24, 2024 • 2 hours**

- How to create a social media content marketing plan for your nonprofit
- The eight types of top-performing content for social media
- Best practices for sharing website, photo, and video content on social media
- How to create an editorial calendar for your content marketing plan

**3) Social Media Fundraising for Nonprofits  
Presented August 7, 2024 • 2 hours**

- How to effectively promote fundraising campaigns on social media
- How to use social media to grow your email and text subscribers
- An introduction to social advertising for fundraising and capturing leads
- How to effectively use Facebook and Instagram Fundraising Tools
