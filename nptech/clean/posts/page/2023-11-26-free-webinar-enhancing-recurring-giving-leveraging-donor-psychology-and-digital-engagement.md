[This interactive webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/3892613545877252957) delves into the strategies for nurturing and growing recurring giving programs. By blending insights from donor psychology with the latest digital engagement techniques, we will explore how to create personalized and meaningful experiences for donors. This session emphasizes the importance of understanding donor motivations and using technology to foster lasting relationships and support.

Learning Objectives:

- Deepening Donor Relationships for Recurring Support: Strategies to understand and connect with donors at a personal level to encourage sustained giving.
- Digital Storytelling for Recurring Donations: Techniques for using digital platforms to tell impactful stories that resonate with donors and inspire regular contributions.
- Optimizing Technology for Recurring Giving Campaigns: Best practices for leveraging digital tools to facilitate easy, engaging, and memorable recurring donation experiences.

Participants will gain insights into creating a nurturing environment for donors, encouraging them to commit to regular support through strategic use of psychological understanding and digital tools.
