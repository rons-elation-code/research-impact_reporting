## 2021 Global NGO Technology Survey Promo Text

The promo text below can be used to help promote the survey to your email list and social media communities, however using your own voice and tone will likely work best!

###### **Email:**

The **[2021 Open Data Project](http://www.nptechforgood.com/open-data-project/)** is based on the survey responses from the Global NGO Technology Survey. Published in multiple languages, the survey seeks to gain a better understanding of how NGOs, nonprofits, and charities worldwide use technology for digital marketing and fundraising. Survey questions cover website and email communications, online fundraising tools, social media, and productivity and security software.

The **[2021 Global NGO Technology Survey](https://survey.alchemer.com/s3/6079524/2021-Global-NGO-Technology-Survey)** is 100% anonymous and once submitted, you will be provided instant access to the data which can be browsed online or downloaded into Excel, PowerPoint, a PDF, and/or Word. The anonymized survey data is available in real-time by region and country and as a global average.

The more survey respondents we have, the better the data – so please volunteer 6-12 minutes of your time and take the survey!

**[[Take the Survey](https://survey.alchemer.com/s3/6079524/2021-Global-NGO-Technology-Survey)]**

##### Social Media:

The 2021 Open Data Project provides digital marketing and fundraising benchmarks for charitable organizations worldwide. Learn more. Take the survey. Access the data: **<http://www.nptechforgood.com/open-data-project>**

The 2021 Open Data Project is based on the survey responses from the 2021 Global NGO Technology Survey. The anonymized survey data is available in real-time by region and country and as a global average. To begin, learn more and take the survey: **<http://www.nptechforgood.com/open-data-project>**

Open data is the idea that some data should be freely available to everyone to use and republish as they wish. For the NGO/NPO/charity sector, Nonprofit Tech for Good has launched the 2021 Open Data Project. Learn more and participate: **<http://www.nptechforgood.com/open-data-project>**
