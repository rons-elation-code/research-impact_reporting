**1) Earn a Certificate from Nonprofit Tech for Good**

Nonprofit Tech for Good offers [three low-cost certificate programs for nonprofit professionals](https://www.nptechforgood.com/certificate-programs/). The curriculum covers digital marketing, online fundraising, social media, and artificial intelligence (AI). To earn the certificates, you can attend the webinars live or view the recordings – or a combination of both.

---

**2) Attend a Webinar**

Nonprofit Tech for Good regularly hosts [free and low-cost webinars](https://us12.mailchimp.com/mctx/clicks?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.nptechforgood.com%2Fwebinars%2F&h=f9e415cc9928e806f78465904811325d33b9d6a1c1c8d7fc346a8eb89431d531&v=1&xid=0b51fa2020&uid=52771941&pool=contact_facing&subject=Thank+you+for+subscribing+to+Nonprofit+Tech+for+Good%21) for the nonprofit sector. If you can not attend a webinar live, you can still register in order to be emailed a link to the recording within 24-48 hours of the end of the webinar.
