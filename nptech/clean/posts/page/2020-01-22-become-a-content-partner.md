### Benefits

In addition to building your brand recognition within the Nonprofit Tech for Good Community, sponsored posts receive 2,000-8,000 visits over a 12-month period with click-throughs to your website ranging from 3-10%. Promoted content resources receive between 400-800 clicks over a 4-week promotion period.

Content partnerships are a unique sponsorship opportunity and [more affordable and better targeted than digital advertising](https://www.webfx.com/social-media/pricing/how-much-does-social-media-advertising-cost/). *Please note that promoted content resources must alternate between gated and ungated.*
