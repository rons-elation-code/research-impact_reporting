# 2026 Social Media Statistics for Nonprofits

> A supplemental post to 101 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Best Practices for Nonprofits, these 100+ statistics can guide your nonprofit in creating and maintaining a successful social media strategy.

A supplemental post to [101 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Best Practices for Nonprofits](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/), the statistics listed below can guide your nonprofit in creating and maintaining a successful social media strategy.

---

## Facebook

- Facebook has 3.05 billion monthly active users of which 65% access the site daily and spend an average of 40 minutes per day on the platform. [[HootSuite](https://blog.hootsuite.com/facebook-statistics/)]
- By 2027, Facebook will reach 75% of the world’s population and is currently the world’s third most trafficked website after Google and YouTube. [[Similarweb](https://www.similarweb.com/top-websites/)]
- 62% of its users worldwide are millennials and Gen Z. [[Statista](https://www.statista.com/statistics/187549/facebook-distribution-of-users-age-group-usa/)]
- 93% of nonprofits use Facebook Pages. [[2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)]
- On average, organic posts reach 2.2% of their followers on Facebook. [[Social Status](https://www.socialstatus.io/insights/social-media-benchmarks/facebook-organic-reach-rate-benchmark/)]
- Nonprofits post an average of 5.5 times per week to their Facebook Page. [[Rival IQ Social Media Industry Benchmark Report](https://www.rivaliq.com/blog/social-media-industry-benchmark-report/#title-nonprofits)]
- Nonprofits have an average engagement rate of 0.046% on Facebook. [[Rival IQ Social Media Industry Benchmark Report](https://www.rivaliq.com/blog/social-media-industry-benchmark-report/#title-nonprofits)]
- 53% of nonprofits spend on social media advertising. Of those that spend on social media ads, 98% spend on Facebook. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- Facebook/Meta has an average cost per lead of $3.20 compared to $17.40 on TikTok. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- Facebook/Meta results in an average return on ad spend (ROAS) of $0.48 for fundraising ads compared to $0.03 on TikTok. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- The average cost per donation via Facebook Ads/Meta is $106 compared to $1,040 on TikTok. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- For every 1,000 email addresses, nonprofits have an average of 1,041 Facebook fans. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 37% of nonprofits use Facebook Fundraising Tools. 35% of nonprofits raised more money than they expected while 23% raised less. 32% raised what they expected. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 97% of all Facebook fundraising revenue is donated through Facebook Fundraisers. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- The average Fundraiser has three gifts that average $36 USD — $108 USD in total. [*[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/ "https://mrbenchmarks.com/")*]
- Giving through Facebook Fundraising Tools resulted in 0.2% of all online revenue for nonprofits in 2024 – down from 1.1% in 2023. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- Facebook fundraisers who are thanked by nonprofits during the fundraising campaign raise 35% more than those who are not. [[Give Panel](https://givepanel.com/)]
- 22% of nonprofits utilize UTM codes to monitor website traffic from Facebook/social media. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]

## LinkedIn

- LinkedIn has 1 billion registered users of which 49% are monthly active users and 16% are daily active users. Monthly users spend an average of 17 minutes per month on LinkedIn. [[The Social Shepherd](https://thesocialshepherd.com/blog/linkedin-statistics)]
- 44% of U.S. adults who use LinkedIn earn over $75,000 a year and 51% have a college degree. [[Sprinklr](https://www.sprinklr.com/blog/linkedin-demographics/)]
- 36% of LinkedIn users are millennials, 27% are Gen X, 26% are Gen Z, and 9% are baby boomers. [[HootSuite](https://blog.hootsuite.com/linkedin-demographics-for-business/)]
- There are 2.2 million nonprofits on LinkedIn and 22 million nonprofit professionals. [[LinkedIn](https://nonprofit.linkedin.com/)]
- 27% of nonprofits worldwide have an official policy to allow staff to work on their LinkedIn Profiles during work hours. [[Global NGO Technology Report](http://www.nptechforgood.com/global-ngo-technology-report/)]
- 81% of nonprofits use LinkedIn Pages. [[2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)]
- 68% of nonprofits post less than once weekly, 15% post once weekly, 8% post once every other day, 6% post once daily, and 3% post twice or more daily. [[Global NGO Technology Report](http://www.nptechforgood.com/global-ngo-technology-report/)]
- On average, posts reach 2.9% of their followers on LinkedIn. [[Social Status](https://www.socialstatus.io/insights/social-media-benchmarks/linkedin-organic-reach-rate-benchmark/)]
- Nonprofits have an average engagement rate of 1.91% on LinkedIn. [[HootSuite Social Media Trends Report](https://www.hootsuite.com/en-gb/research/social-trends)]
- 53% of nonprofits spend on social media advertising. Of those that spend on social media ads, 17% spend on LinkedIn. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- For every 1,000 email addresses, nonprofits have an average of 58 LinkedIn followers. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 42% of US donors use LinkedIn to research nonprofits to support and 26% discover donation opportunities on LinkedIn. [[Classy](https://learn.classy.org/rs/673-DCU-558/images/donor-engagement-report-linkedin.pdf)]

## Instagram

- Instagram has 3 billion monthly active users of which 500 million access the site daily and spend an average of 24 minutes per day on the platform. [[The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/news/784784/instagram-3-billion-monthly-users-reels-dms-updates)]
- 62% of Instagram users are between the ages of 18 to 34. [[Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/instagram-users)]
- 85% of nonprofits worldwide use Instagram. [[2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)]
- On average, posts reach 15.3% of their followers on Instagram. [[Social Status](https://www.socialstatus.io/insights/social-media-benchmarks/instagram-organic-reach-rate-benchmark/)]
- Nonprofits post an average of 4.9 times per week to their Instagram account. [[Rival IQ Social Media Industry Benchmark Report](https://www.rivaliq.com/blog/social-media-industry-benchmark-report/#title-nonprofits)]
- Nonprofits have an average engagement rate of 0.623% on Instagram. [[Rival IQ Social Media Industry Benchmark Report](https://www.rivaliq.com/blog/social-media-industry-benchmark-report/#title-nonprofits)]
- 53% of nonprofits spend on social media advertising. Of those that spend on social media ads, 47% spend on Instagram. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- For every 1,000 email addresses, nonprofits have an average of 251 Instagram followers. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 13% of nonprofits use Instagram Fundraising Tools. 10% of nonprofits raised more money than they expected from Instagram Fundraising Tools while 57% raised less. 33% raised what they expected. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- Instagram is the most commonly used platform for nonprofit influencer campaigns. Of those nonprofits that engage in influencer campaigns, 94% work with influencers on Instagram. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]

## Threads

- Threads reached 100 million active users in 4 days – faster than any app in history – 12X times faster than ChatGPT and 55X faster than TikTok. [[Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/threads-users)]
- 2% of nonprofits in the U.S. use Threads. [[2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Surv](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)[ey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)]
- Threads has 400 million monthly active users and 141 million daily active users. [[Backlinko](https://backlinko.com/threads-users)]
- 62% of nonprofits active on X have started building a presence on other emerging platforms. Of those, 88% are experimenting with Threads, 84% with BlueSky, 6% with Mastodon, and 5% with Discord. [[M+R Benchmarks Report](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]

## Bluesky

- 4% of nonprofits in the U.S. use Bluesky. [[2025 Nonprofit Tech for Good Surv](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)[ey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/04/15/announcing-the-2025-nonprofit-tech-for-good-survey/)]

- 60% of Bluesky users are male and one-third are between the ages of 18 and 24. [[Exploding Topics](https://explodingtopics.com/blog/bluesky-users)]

## TikTok
