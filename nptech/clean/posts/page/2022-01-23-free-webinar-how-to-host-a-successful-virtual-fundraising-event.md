A hybrid fundraising event can enhance the typical fundraising experience for your donors, especially when hosted using the latest fundraising technology. In this webinar, see and experience cutting-edge tools to make your events more enjoyable for your donors and less work for your organization.

Learn what it takes to make your hybrid fundraising event a success from planning, to execution, to follow up. How can the latest technology help me collect more donations? What does it take to put on a great show for your donors? How can you touch the hearts of your guests even if they are joining from a remote part of the world?

[**This webinar**](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/1121733178369242380) is designed to deliver an actionable blueprint for fundraising success based on real-life examples of nonprofit hybrid fundraisers. Topics include:

- Best practices for hosting hybrid fundraisers and how to ensure a successful, engaging event
- How to enhance donor experience with auto check-in, auto-checkout, magic bid paddles, mobile bidding, and more
- See an example of a live, real-time bidding war between online and in-person guests
- The technology needed to live-stream a hybrid fundraiser, from simple to advanced
- Marketing tips to maximize event registration and generate excitement for your upcoming event
- How to use your event data to follow up with donors and attendees to ensure continued success with virtual events
