# [Webinar Recording] Social Media Content Marketing for Nonprofits

> With a focus on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Threads, and TikTok for nonprofits, attendees of this two-hour webinar will learn how to integrate content marketing into their social media strategy and current best practices to increase engagement on social media.

**Presented: July 24, 2024**  
**Duration: 2 hours**  
**Cost: $30 (USD)**  
**Presented by: [Heather Mansfield](http://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/), Founder of Nonprofit Tech for Good**

---

Social media can be an effective communication tool for nonprofits, but only if nonprofits understand that creating well-written and visually appealing content is essential to acquiring new supporters from social media.

With a focus on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and Threads for nonprofits, attendees of this [two-hour webinar](https://buy.stripe.com/3cs4jMfbL2PD4Tu5kv) will learn how to integrate content marketing into their social media strategy and current best practices to increase engagement on social media. Topics include:

- How to create a social media content marketing plan for your nonprofit
- The eight types of top-performing content for social media
- How often nonprofits should post on social media
- Best practices for sharing website, photo, and video content on social media
- How to create an editorial calendar for your content marketing plan
