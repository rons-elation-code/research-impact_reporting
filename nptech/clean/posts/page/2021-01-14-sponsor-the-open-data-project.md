# Sponsor the Open Data Project

Benefits: Your logo and website are featured on all pages of the Open Data Project. Your company/organization is featured in quarterly blog posts about the data. [**Open Data Project**](http://www.nptechforgood.com/open-data-project/) sponsorships are a unique opportunity to reach tens of thousands of nonprofit professionals worldwide. The cost to sponsor the Open Data Project for 12 months is $1,200.

#### **Benefits:**

1. Your logo and website are featured on all pages of the [Open Data Project](http://www.nptechforgood.com/open-data-project/).
2. Your company/organization is featured in quarterly [blog posts](http://www.nptechforgood.com/category/open-data-project/) about the data.
3. You have the option to write [one sponsored post](http://www.nptechforgood.com/publish-a-sponsored-blog-post/) (at no cost) using the data for Nonprofit Tech for Good.
4. The ability to request exclusive configurations of the data.

**To learn more about becoming a sponsor for the Open Data Project, please [contact Heather Mansfield](http://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/).**
