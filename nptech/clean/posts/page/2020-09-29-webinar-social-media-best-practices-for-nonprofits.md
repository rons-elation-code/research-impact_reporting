# [Webinar] Social Media for Nonprofits

> From embracing Facebook & Instagram Fundraising Tools to budgeting for social media ads, this two-hour webinar will focus on what’s new and next in social media so that your nonprofit can embrace being an early adopter.

**Date: Wednesday, November 6, 2024**  
**Time: 1 pm ET / 10 am PT (2 hours)  
Cost: $40 (USD)**  
**Presented by: [Heather Mansfield](http://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/), Founder of Nonprofit Tech for Good**

---

Social media is in a constant state of change and without regular education about emerging trends, your nonprofit could be wasting valuable time and resources implementing outdated best practices.

From embracing Facebook & Instagram Fundraising Tools to budgeting for social media ads, [this two-hour webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/6817378250474594655) will focus on what’s new and next in social media so that your nonprofit can embrace being an early adopter. Topics include:

•  Current best practices for using Facebook Pages and Instagram  
•  What’s new in Facebook and Instagram Fundraising Tools  
•  How to effectively use LinkedIn Pages and Profiles  
•  An introduction to Threads for nonprofits  
•  Why nonprofits need a budget for social advertising
