A supplemental post to [101 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Best Practices for Nonprofits](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/), the website statistics listed below can guide your nonprofit in creating and maintaining a website that maximizes the user experience and fundraising success.

---

## Website Design

- 94% of consumers/donors say that their first impression of a brand is based on its website’s design. [[Web FX](https://www.webfx.com/blog/web-design/website-statistics/)]
- 25% of website visitors will abandon a website if it takes more than four seconds to load. [[Website Builder Expert](https://www.websitebuilderexpert.com/building-websites/website-load-time-statistics/)]
- 58% of nonprofits use WordPress.org as their content management system (CMS) for their website (7% use Wix, 6% use Squarespace, and 5% use Drupal). [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 75% use the .org domain for their primary website and email communications. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 94% of nonprofits have a website that is optimized for mobile browsing. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 22% of nonprofits have websites designed for those with visual and hearing disabilities. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 20% have a website that is available in more than one language. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 68% have redesigned their website within the last three years. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 28% use email subscribe popups on their website. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]

## Website Traffic

- Nonprofits have an average of 12,708 website visitors per month. [[NextAfter](https://www.nextafter.com/blog/3-lessons-from-analyzing-the-key-metrics-of-155-nonprofits/)]
- Nonprofits average a 60-70% bounce rate – a good bounce rate is between 40% or below – indicating that that improvements are needed to engage visitors and encourage them to explore more of your website. [[Impact SEO](https://impactseo.co/resources/website-analytics-for-nonprofits-5-metrics-that-will-help-you-build-success/)]
- Mobile users represented 53% of all visits to nonprofit websites, with 47% of traffic from users on desktop devices. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- Organic traffic (website traffic generated by unpaid search results) generates 44% of all nonprofit website visits. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- The top three sources of website traffic are direct / email (22%), organic search (17%), and social media (16%). [[HubSpot](https://blog.hubspot.com/blog/tabid/6307/bid/5092/how-many-visitors-should-your-site-get.aspx)]

## Website Fundraising

- Visitors on desktop devices make up the majority of donation transactions (55%) and revenue (70%). [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- The average gift made on desktop devices is $145; for mobile users, the average gift is $76. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 1.5% of website visitors make a donation, generating an average of $1.29 per visitor. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 68% of online donors most trust websites and email addresses that use the .org domain. [[Global Trends in Giving Report](http://www.nptechforgood.com/global-trends-in-giving-report/)]

## Website Security

- 68% of nonprofits have a website security plan in place. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 84% of nonprofits have an SSL. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 27% of nonprofits worldwide have experienced a cyberattack. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
