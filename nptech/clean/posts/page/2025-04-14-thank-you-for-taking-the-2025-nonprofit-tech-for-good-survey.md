The [2023 Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/Nonprofit-Tech-for-Good-Report-Final2-2023.pdf) report is based on the survey responses of 1,732 nonprofit professionals. The survey was completed by nonprofit professionals from 116 countries.
