A supplemental post to [101 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Best Practices for Nonprofits](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/), the statistics listed below can guide your nonprofit in creating and maintaining a successful online fundraising strategy.

## Online Fundraising Strategy

- 33% of donors say that email is the tool that most inspires them to give (29% social media, website 17%, print 9%). [[2025 Online Donor Feedback Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/02/15/the-communication-giving-preferences-of-641-online-donors/)]
- 72% of individuals say that the presence of a charity rating badge increases their likelihood of giving. [[Great Nonprofits](https://greatnonprofits.org/)]
- 84% of online donors are more likely to give if their donations are matched by their employer. [[Double the Donation](https://doublethedonation.com/)]
- The average donor retention rate in 2024 was 45% – down 2.6% from 2023. [[Fundraising Effectiveness Project](https://data.givingtuesday.org/fep-report/)]
- Frequent, consistent communication with online donors results in a 41.5% increase in revenue. [[NextAfter](https://www.nextafter.com/blog/beginners-guide-to-donor-cultivation/)]
- The average return on ad spend (ROAS) for fundraising ads on Meta platforms is $0.48 USD. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 93% of nonprofits believe that a strong brand identity has a positive impact on donor engagement. [[99designs](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2022/07/16/infographic-how-good-design-impacts-nonprofit-digital-marketing-fundraising/)]
- 75% of donors look for concrete information about a nonprofit’s achievements before making a decision to give. [[Root Cause](https://rootcause.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/Informed_Giving_Full_Report.pdf)]

## Online Giving

- Online giving accounts for 13.4% of small nonprofits’ total revenue, 8.3% for medium nonprofits, and 4.1% for large nonprofits. [[2024 Trends in Giving Report by Blackbaud](https://institute.blackbaud.com/resources/2024-trends)]
- The average gift made on desktop devices is $145; for mobile users, the average gift is $76. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 45% of online donations were made on a mobile device in 2024. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- The average nonprofit raises $1.11 per email contact – $6.15 for small nonprofits, $0.88 for large nonprofits. [[Neon One Nonprofit Email Report](https://neonone.com/resources/nonprofit-email-report/)]
- 25% of annual online revenue is raised in December and donations made on December 29, December 30, and December 31 account for 10% of annual online revenue. [[Neon One](https://neonone.com/resources/blog/year-end-giving-statistics/)]
- 46% of online donors give tribute gifts to family and friends. Donors are most interested in giving tribute gifts as memorials (43%), birthday gifts (25%), and for religious holidays (10%). [[2025 Online Donor Feedback Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/02/15/the-communication-giving-preferences-of-641-online-donors/)]
- The average amount raised by peer-to-peer fundraisers is $244. [[Raisely](https://www.raisely.com/blog/the-peer-to-peer-fundraising-playbook-2025/)]
- GivingTuesday 2024 raised $3.6 billion – a 16% increase from GivingTuesday 2023’s total of $3.1 billion. [[GivingTuesday](https://www.givingtuesday.org/blog/givingtuesday-2024-record-breaking-results/)]

## Monthly Giving

- The average monthly gift is $24. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- Monthly giving accounts for 31% of all online revenue. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 16% of nonprofits use pop-ups to prompt one-time donors to become monthly donors. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/02/01/announcing-the-2023-nonprofit-tech-for-good-report/)]
- 91% of donors use a credit card for their monthly gifts, 8% use ACH, and 1% use PayPal. [[Neon One Recurring Giving Report](https://neonone.com/resources/recurring-giving-report/)]

## Website Fundraising

- Visitors on desktop devices make up the majority of donation transactions (55%) and revenue (70%). [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 1.5% of website visitors made a donation in 2024, generating an average of $1.29 per visitor. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/charts/website-performance)]
- 68% of online donors most trust websites and email addresses that use the .org domain. [[Global Trends in Giving Report](http://www.nptechforgood.com/global-trends-in-giving-report/)]
- 55% of online donors will opt-in to pay credit card processing fees. [[GoFundMe Pro](https://pro.gofundme.com/c/blog/supporters-cover-donation-fees/)]
- 72% of individuals say that the presence of a [charity rating badge](https://secure.kidneyfund.org/site/SPageNavigator/df_id/onetime75.html?s_src=mainform&s_subsrc=mainform_headeronetime&_ga=2.188730280.1481796608.1593991943-1802472686.1593991943) on a donation page increases their likelihood of giving.

## Email Fundraising

- 33% of donors say that email is the tool that most inspires them to give (29% social media, website 17%, print 9%). [[2025 Online Donor Feedback Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/02/15/the-communication-giving-preferences-of-641-online-donors/)]
- 48% of donors cite email as their preferred method of hearing updates and receiving fundraising appeals from the organization. Direct mail was the second most preferred channel at 21%, followed by social media (17%), text messaging (8%), and phone calls (2%). [[Neon One Nonprofit Email Report](https://neonone.com/resources/nonprofit-email-report/)]
- Email messaging resulted in 11% of all online revenue in 2024 – down from 14% in 2023. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- For every 1,000 fundraising messages sent, nonprofits raised $58 in 2024.  [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- Donor retention increases by 29% for offline donors when you have their email address. [[NextAfter](https://www.nextafter.com/blog/complete-guide-email-acquisition/)]

## Social Media Fundraising

- Giving through Facebook Fundraising Tools resulted in 0.2% of all online revenue for nonprofits in 2024 – down from 1.1% in 2023. [[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/)]
- 38% of online donors have donated to a nonprofit through Facebook. Of those, 70% say that they will do so again in the future. [[2025 Online Donor Feedback Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/02/15/the-communication-giving-preferences-of-641-online-donors/)]
- 12% of online donors have donated to a nonprofit through Instagram. Of those, 83% say that they will do so again in the future. [[2025 Online Donor Feedback Survey](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2025/02/15/the-communication-giving-preferences-of-641-online-donors/)]
- The average Fundraiser has three gifts that average $36 USD — $108 USD in total. [*[M+R Benchmarks](https://mrbenchmarks.com/ "https://mrbenchmarks.com/")*]

## Online Donors

- 54% of US households donate to charity. [[YouGov](https://today.yougov.com/society/articles/43435-half-americans-donate-money-charity-past-year-poll)]
- 51% of donations are made by women and 63% of GivingTuesday donations are made by women. [[Women’s Philanthropy Institute](https://scholarworks.iupui.edu/server/api/core/bitstreams/ae8f3d6d-5cce-4a82-b5f5-3d3d750e9894/content)]
- 88% of the Silent Generation gives to charity, donating an annual average of $1,367 across 6.2 organizations. [[AARP](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/08/04/6-generations-of-giving-who-gives-the-most-and-how-they-prefer-to-give/)]
- 72% give to charity, donating an annual average of $1,212 across an average of 4.5 organizations. [[AARP](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/08/04/6-generations-of-giving-who-gives-the-most-and-how-they-prefer-to-give/)]
- Gen X donates an average annual gift of $732 across four charities and leads in annual volunteer hours served. [[Nonprofit Pro](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/08/04/6-generations-of-giving-who-gives-the-most-and-how-they-prefer-to-give/)]
- 84% give to charity, donating an annual average of $481 across 3.3 organizations. [[AARP](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/08/04/6-generations-of-giving-who-gives-the-most-and-how-they-prefer-to-give/)]
- 8% of donors give stock or mutual funds to nonprofits and 4% donate to Donor-Advised Funds (DAFs). [[Global Trends in Giving Report](http://www.nptechforgood.com/global-trends-in-giving-report/)]
- 66% of donors worldwide volunteer with nonprofits. Of those, 73% donate to the nonprofits they volunteer for. [[Global Trends in Giving Report](http://www.nptechforgood.com/global-trends-in-giving-report/)]
- Affluent households give an average of $34,917 to charity. [[Bank of America Study of Philanthropy](https://philanthropy.iupui.edu/news-events/news/_news/2023/Charitable%20Giving%20by%20Affluent%20Households%20Above%20Pre-Pandemic%20Levels,%20Finds%202023%20Bank%20of%20America%20Study%20of%20Philanthropy.html)]
