# 2026 Blogging Statistics for Nonprofits

> 48% of nonprofits worldwide have a blog or news section on their website. Use these blogging stats to guide your nonprofit in creating and maintaining a blogging strategy that maximizes traffic to your website and inspires readers to get more involved with your nonprofit.

A supplemental post to [101 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Best Practices for Nonprofits](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/), the blogging statistics listed below can guide your nonprofit in creating and maintaining a blogging strategy that maximizes traffic to your website and inspires readers to get more involved with your nonprofit.

---

## Blogging Strategy

- 48% of nonprofits worldwide have a blog or news section on their website. [[Global NGO Technology Report](https://www.nptechforgood.com/global-ngo-technology-report/)]
- 79% of marketers name blogging as their most effective strategy for content marketing. [[Classy.org](https://www.classy.org/)]
- Brands that prioritize blogging see 13 times more ROI than brands that don’t. [[HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/)]
- Brands with blogs produce an average of 67% more leads monthly than brands that don’t blog. [[DemandMetric](https://www.demandmetric.com/content/content-marketing-infographic)]
- 43% of bloggers use AI to generate ideas for blog topics, but only 3% use AI to write complete posts. [[Orbit Media](https://www.orbitmedia.com/blog/blogging-statistics/)]
- Less than 10% of nonprofits utilize the sidebar on their blog i.e., no email opt-in or “Donate” button. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/10-blogging-best-practices-for-nonprofits/)]
- Less than 5% of nonprofits embed a call-to-action [blurb, promo graphic + button] within blog content. [[Nonprofit Tech for Good](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/10-blogging-best-practices-for-nonprofits/)]

## Blogging SEO

- Blog posts that are 2,000+ words have the best SEO and social sharing rates. [[Torque](https://torquelaunch.com/)]
- Long-form posts (3,000+ words) generate more than 75% backlinks and 9x more leads than short-form posts (500 words or less). [[BKA](https://www.bkacontent.com/)]
- Websites with active blogs have 434% more indexed pages and 97% more inbound links than those that don’t. [[BKA](https://www.bkacontent.com/)]

## Blog Traffic

- Brands that blog get 55% more website visitors than brands that don’t. [[HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/)]
- Articles with images get 94% more views as opposed to those with no visuals. [[OptinMonster](https://optinmonster.com/)]
- Email drives more traffic to blog posts than social media and SEO. Influencers and paid ads drive the most. [[Orbit Media](https://www.orbitmedia.com/blog/blogging-statistics/)]

## Blog Content

- The average blog post is 1,376 words and takes 3 hours and 51 minutes to write. [[Orbit Media](https://www.orbitmedia.com/blog/blogging-statistics/)]
- How-to articles (76%), lists (55%), and news and trends (47%) are the three most popular types of blog content. [[Orbit Media](https://www.orbitmedia.com/blog/blogging-statistics/)]
- The ideal blog headline length is between six and 13 words / 50 to 60 characters. and numbered lists 206% more clicks than those that do not. [[BKA](https://www.bkacontent.com/)]
- Bloggers who use 7+ images per post are 2.3x more likely to report strong results. [[Orbit Media](https://www.orbitmedia.com/blog/blogging-statistics/)]
- Posts with video experienced a 4x increase in engagement metrics. [[Orbit Media](https://www.orbitmedia.com/blog/blogging-statistics/)]
