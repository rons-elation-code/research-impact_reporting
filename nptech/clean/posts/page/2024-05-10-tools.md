## Chatbots

**•** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/) is a large language model chatbot driven by [generative AI](https://www.zdnet.com/article/what-is-generative-ai-and-why-is-it-so-popular-heres-everything-you-need-to-know/) that can answer questions and assist with tasks such as marketing emails, fundraising appeals, and grant proposals.

**•** [Claude](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) is a [large language model (LLM)](https://www.cloudflare.com/learning/ai/what-is-large-language-model/) and chatbot developed by Anthropic, a startup founded by former OpenAI members. Claude is trained to have natural conversations and is good at summarization, editing, and code-writing. 

**•** [Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com/) is a generative AI digital assistant and chatbot developed by Microsoft. Integrated with Windows 365 and Bing, Copilot can draft written content, function as a research assistant, and improve productivity in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams.

**•**[EMPWRD AI](https://empwrd.ai/) is a chat assistant that challenges bias in AI data by prioritizing inclusivity, empowerment, diverse content generation.

**•** [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/app) is a generative AI chatbot developed by Google. Gemini is designed to understand and respond to a wide range of inputs, including text, images, and code. Like ChatGPT, Claude, and Copilot, Gemini is [multimodal](https://cloud.google.com/use-cases/multimodal-ai), meaning it can reason across different input data types, such as audio, images, and text.

**•** [Jasper](https://www.jasper.ai/templates) is a specialized chatbot for writing marketing content—from blog post outlines to LinkedIn Bios to video descriptions.

**•** [Rallybot AI](https://rallystarter.com/rallybot) writes advocacy campaigns for online petitions and email campaigns that can contact corporate and government decision-makers.

## Fundraising

**•** [AltruAI](https://altru.ai/) empowers users to create and publish professional nonprofit annual reports with the power of AI technology.

**•** [Appeal AI](https://www.funraise.org/appealai) makes it easy to create compelling fundraising appeals for email campaigns, website pages, and social media.

**•** [Avid](https://www.avidai.com/fundraising-intelligence/) offers a free version to a fundraising intelligence dashboard that features annual fundraising benchmarks (the paid version is updated daily).

**•** [Chorus](https://www.chorusai.co/) is AI-powered fundraising software that identifies which messages drive donations, automates content creation (text, images, video), and tracks performance across email, social, ads, direct mail, and phone campaigns.

**•** [Dataro](https://dataro.io/) uses AI to improve fundraising outcomes by using state-of-the-art predictive analytics software that allows nonprofits to assess how and when their donors will give in the future.

**•** [Fundraise Up](https://fundraiseup.com/platform/fundraising-ai/) uses machine learning to process 80+ non-PII data points to customize suggested donation amounts and payment methods to potential new donors that visit your nonprofit’s website.

**•** [Fundwriter](https://www.fundwriter.ai/) uses AI to help you write all things fundraising, including fundraising appeals for different donor segments, personalized thank you letters, and engaging email fundraising campaigns.

**•** [Grantboost](https://www.grantboost.io/) is a grant writing tool powered by AI that drafts funding proposals for specific grant opportunities.

**•** [Hatch](https://hatch.ai/) is a giving intelligence platform that helps you find potential donors, volunteers, and influencers in your network and visualize them into human profiles so you can better understand what inspires them to give and act on behalf of causes they care about.

**•** [Practivated](https://practivated.com/) is the first and only AI-powered coaching platform built to help fundraisers practice conversations, overcome nerves, and take confident action.

## Images & Graphic Design

**•** [aiCarousels](https://www.aicarousels.com/) simplifies the process of creating carousel content for social media.

**•** [Aragon](https://www.aragon.ai/) that transforms selfies into professional headshots for websites and social media.

**•** [DALL·E](https://openai.com/index/dall-e-3/) creates images from text captions for a wide range of concepts expressible in natural language.

**•** [Magic Studio by Canva](https://www.canva.com/magic/) uses generative AI to empower users to create and edit images, graphics, and video content by using text prompts.

**•** [Plus](https://www.plusdocs.com/) allows users to edit and generate presentations for AI Google Slides and PowerPoint in any language.

## Video

**•** [invideo AI](https://invideo.io/ai/) creates videos with text prompts. Type any topic to create a video with script, visuals, subtitles, voiceover, transitions, and music. 

**•** [Opus Clip](https://www.opus.pro/) is a generative AI video tool that repurposes long videos into shorts for Instagram Reels and TikToks in one click.

## Audio

**•** [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/) offers AI-empowered text-to-speech, speech-to-speech, and voice cloning tools that can be used to voice audiobooks, news articles, blog posts, and to create audio content for social media and advertising.

## Website & Email

**•** [AI Email Writer AI](https://mailmeteor.com/tools/ai-email-writer) uses a large language model (LLM) to generate email content based on your instructions—from one-on-one personal emails to fundraising appeals meant for large audiences.

**•** [Elementor AI Website Builder](https://elementor.com/products/ai/) utilizes AI Copilot to edit or create a WordPress website that enhances layout, text, images, calls-to-action, e-commerce, and allows custom code directly within the Elementor Editor.

**•** [Unlayer](https://unlayer.com/) is a drag-and-drop email template designer that utilizes AI for smart headings, image generation, and smart buttons.

**•** [Wix AI Website Builder](https://www.wix.com/ai-website-builder) generates a custom website design by answering simple questions with the Wix chatbot. Ideal for beginners, Wix suggests AI-generated images, text, layouts, and color schemes.

## Social Media

**•** [Buffer AI Assistan](https://buffer.com/ai-assistant)t writes, edits, repurposes, and publishes social media posts for Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Threads, TikTok, and Pinterest.

## Productivity

**•** [Dropbox Dash](https://dash.dropbox.com/) is an AI-powered universal search of connected apps ranging from Asana to Zendesk that allows you to quickly search files, apps, emails, and calendars, and then displays the search results in an easy-to-use dashboard.

**•** [Grammarly](https://app.grammarly.com/) is a generative AI writing assistant that catches typos, missing punctuation, incorrect punctuation, and suggests sentence structures and word alternatives that match the mood and attitude of your nonprofit’s tone of voice.

**•** [Handwrytten](https://www.handwrytten.com/) uses handwriting robots that hold real pens to write out notes in your handwriting and signature.

**•** [Otter AI](https://otter.ai/) transcribes, summarizes, and provides action items for in-person or Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams meetings.

**•** [Shakespeare](https://soapbox.pub/tools/shakespeare) is an AI-powered app builder that runs directly in your web browser. Simply describe the app you want to build, such as a sign-up form, chatroom, or marketplace, and Shakespeare builds it in real time with a live preview system.

## Search

**•** [Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) Grader](https://www.hubspot.com/aeo-grader) by Hubspot reveals how leading AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini interpret your brand. As an example, view the [Nonprofit Tech for Good AEO Report](https://www.hubspot.com/hubfs/aims-aeo-grader/AEO-Grader-Report-2025-11-09_3cc7ea52d3a68184cff20c23f46c1d61bb5097293cc1e21e3572977b316d8fa9.pdf).

**•** [Google AI Overviews](https://blog.google/products/search/generative-ai-google-search-may-2024/) are a core Google Search feature that include links to learn more and are powered by Google’s Gemini.

**•** [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/) is an AI-powered answer engine that summarizes search results and prominently features sources. 

**•** [SearchGPT](https://openai.com/index/searchgpt-prototype/) is an AI Search that provides fast and timely answers with clear and relevant sources.
