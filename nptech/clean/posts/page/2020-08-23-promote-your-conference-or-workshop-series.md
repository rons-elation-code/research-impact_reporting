Nonprofit Tech for Good will promote your conference or workshop series (related to technology and/or fundraising) in our our e-newsletter and to our social communities in exchange for sponsorship. This opportunity is open to nonprofit technology companies and bloggers that want to expand their reach and secure more event attendees.

### **Cost:**

The fee for promoting your event is $500 per event. The fee includes a $50 boost on Facebook.

### **Where and how often your content is promoted:**

**Weekly Newsletter****:** Your event is featured in [**our e-newsletter**](https://mailchi.mp/nptechforgood/news) twice.  
**Twitter:**Your event is tweeted/retweeted from [**our Twitter account**](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs) 7-10 times (depending upon its popularity).  
**Facebook:**Your event is shared once on our [**Facebook Page**](https://www.facebook.com/nonprofitorgs) and [**Facebook Group**](https://www.facebook.com/groups/nptechforgood).  
**LinkedIn:** Your event is shared once on our [**LinkedIn Page**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/nonprofitorgs) and once from [**Heather Mansfield’s personal LinkedIn Profile**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/heathermansfield/). Your event is also shared to our LinkedIn Groups (when relevant): [**Social Media for Nonprofit Organizations**](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/1172477)**,**[**Online Fundraising for Nonprofit Organizations**](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/3694133)**,**[**Emerging Trends for Nonprofit Organizations**](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/12078228)

### **To request your event bed promoted** **Nonprofit Tech for Good****, please [contact Heather Mansfield](http://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/).**
