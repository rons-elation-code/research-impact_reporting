Sponsoring a webinar for Nonprofit Tech for Good is an excellent way to grow your brand recognition and provide valuable training to nonprofit professionals. The number of webinar registrants varies from 500-1,000 depending on the topic, and on average, 30% of registrants attend live and 70% register in order to receive the recording of the webinar.

### **Benefits**

Sponsors receive the first name, last name, title, organization name, country, and email address of registrants, and sponsors are expected to send the recording within 48 hours of the end of the webinar.

### Where Your Sponsored Webinar is Promoted

- **On Nonprofit Tech for Good:** Your sponsored webinar is listed on our [Webinars page](http://www.nptechforgood.com/webinars/), featured in a post on Nonprofit Tech for Good, and listed in the [Webinar Archive](http://www.nptechforgood.com/nonprofit-tech-for-good-webinar-archive/) for one year.
- **Our Weekly Newsletter****:** Your sponsored webinar is featured in [our newsletter](https://subscribepage.io/HOjlsE) (12,000 subscribers) for 5-6 weeks depending upon our content schedule and featured in one standalone email to our newsletter subscribers 3-4 weeks before your webinar.
- **Facebook:**Your sponsored post is shared once to our [Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/nonprofitorgs) (110,000 followers) and once to our [Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/nptechforgood) (3,200 members).
- **LinkedIn:** Your sponsored post is shared once to our [LinkedIn Page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/nonprofitorgs) (23,000 followers) and to our LinkedIn Groups (when relevant): [Social Media for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/1172477) (200,000 members), [Online Fundraising for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/3694133) (61,000 members), [Website & Email Marketing for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/12078228/) (3,100 members), [Artificial Intelligence for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/14431695/) (1,700 members).
- **Instagram:** Your sponsored post is shared once to our [Instagram Account](https://www.instagram.com/nonprofitorgs/) (77,000 followers).
- **X:**Your sponsored post is posted/reposted to [our X account](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs) (740,000 followers) 4-8 times depending upon its popularity.
- **Threads:** Your sponsored post is posted once to our [Threads profile](https://www.threads.net/@nonprofitorgs) (11,000 followers).
- **Bluesky:** Your resource is posted once to our [Bluesky account](https://bsky.app/profile/nonprofitorgs.bsky.social) (3,600 followers).
- **Pinterest:** Your sponsored webinars is pinned once to our [Pinterest profile](https://www.pinterest.com/nonprofitorgs/) (25,000 followers).

### **Presentation Guidelines**

- Webinar presentations can not be demos of your product or service. Webinars must be focused on a topic that is useful to registrants whether they use your product or not. You can interweave your product or service throughout the webinar, but it can not be the focus of your presentation.
- Your presentation should be 30-45 minutes in length.
- **[Heather Mansfield](http://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/)** will present a short housekeeping introduction, and if you prefer, will be available to monitor the Q&A during the webinar and to facilitate a live Q&A at the end of the webinar.

Nonprofit Tech for Good and the services we offer would not exist without the support of our sponsors. [Thank you to our 2026 sponsors](https://www.nptechforgood.com/thank-you-to-our-sponsors/)!
