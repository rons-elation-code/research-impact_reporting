**Thank you for registering for the [Certificate in Digital Marketing & Fundraising program](https://www.nptechforgood.com/earn-a-certificate-in-digital-marketing-fundraising-from-nonprofit-tech-for-good/)!**

You will soon receive a confirmation email from GoToWebinar that includes your login information and the webinar schedule for the program. If you do not receive this email, [please contact Heather Mansfield via email](https://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/) as soon as possible to ensure that there is not a typo in your registration.

You will also receive webinar reminders from GoToWebinar one day and one hour before each webinar. Please visit [GoToWebinar’s Attendee System Check](https://support.goto.com/webinar/system-check-attendee) for information on how to join and attend the webinars.

A reminder that you can attend the webinars live or watch the recordings – or a combination of both. A link to the recording and a comprehensive set of notes are sent within 24 hours after each live webinar is over.

**To earn and claim your certificate:**

1. View all three webinars (6 hours of training).
2. [Request your certificate via email from Heather Mansfield](https://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/). Please include the passphrase presented in each webinar and how you would like your name featured on the certificate.
3. Within 3-4 business days, you will be sent a PDF of your certificate for printing and a link to your certificate in image format for [easy sharing on LinkedIn](https://www.nptechforgood.com/certificate-programs/how-to-add-nonprofit-tech-for-good-certificates-to-your-linkedin-profile/) and other social media.
