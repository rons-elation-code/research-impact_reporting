## Certificate in Digital Marketing & Fundraising

Those who register and attend all three webinars in the series (Website & Email, Online Fundraising, and Social Media) will earn a **Certificate of Completion in Digital Marketing & Fundraising** from Nonprofit Tech for Good. The webinars only cost $20 each so that nonprofit professionals worldwide can earn the certificate without having to make a significant financial investment.

To earn the certificate, simply register for and attend all three webinars, save your receipts, and then request your certificate at the end of the third webinar. If you can not attend the webinar live, you will be sent a link to the recording and a set of notes 24-48 hours after the end of the webinar.
