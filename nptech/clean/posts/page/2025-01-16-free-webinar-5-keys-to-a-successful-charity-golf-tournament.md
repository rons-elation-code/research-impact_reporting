Charity golf tournaments are a viable option for nonprofits to elevate their reach, forge and strengthen relationships, and raise mission-critical funds. Successful tournaments require careful planning, strategic partnerships, and the right tools.

[Join us for an informative, fast-paced webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/2509291587944556891) that draws on insights from thousands of successful charity golf tournaments to introduce these five key points of golf fundraising:

- **Tournament Basics:** Find out how to set goals, outline a budget, and determine a planning timeline.
- **Sponsorships:** From building attractive packages to effectively partnering with businesses, learn strategies that help boost your tournament’s sponsorship outcomes.
- **Marketing:** Get cost-effective tips for promoting your event to ensure a full field and maximum impact.
- **Event Day**: Hear best practices for a seamless, memorable experience that keeps golfers and sponsors coming back.
- **Revenue Enhancers:** Get ideas to help your tournament raise the most money possible for your mission.

Nonprofit event planners and fundraising professionals of all experience levels will walk away with practical advice, tech tools, and strategies to help tee up your organization for golf fundraising success.
