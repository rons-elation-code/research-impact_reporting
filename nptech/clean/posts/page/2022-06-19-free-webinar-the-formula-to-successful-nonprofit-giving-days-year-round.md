Cause awareness and giving days are 24-hour (or longer) fundraising events that can be powerful moments throughout the year. These days are aimed at rallying communities together to maximize awareness, engagement, and donations.

- Giving Day trends and benchmarks – why they’ve grown in popularity.
- How to incorporate Giving Days into your year-round fundraising strategy – not just Giving Tuesday!
- How to raise awareness for your Giving Day – tried and true customer success stories.
- Best practices for online Giving Day fundraising pages
- Q&A —ask us anything!
