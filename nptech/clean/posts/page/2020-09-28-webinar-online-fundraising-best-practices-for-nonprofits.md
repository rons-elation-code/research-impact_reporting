Customized for small nonprofits with limited time and financial resources, [this two-hour webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/2644871260886175062) will highlight current trends in online fundraising. Online donations make up an average of 18% of a small nonprofit’s annual budget and that number will continue to grow in the coming years.

It’s crucial that your nonprofit knows the fundamentals of launching and maintaining successful online fundraising campaigns as well as knowledge about emerging online fundraising trends in order to prepare for the future. Topics include:

- Today’s must-haves in an online fundraising platform
- Best practices for donation pages
- How to improve your monthly giving and tribute giving programs
- Why and how to launch a donor retention strategy
- An exploration of emerging trends in online fundraising
