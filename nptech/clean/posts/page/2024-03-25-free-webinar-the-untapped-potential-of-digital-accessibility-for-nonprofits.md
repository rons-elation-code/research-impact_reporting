Making sure your website is easy to use is not just a regulatory requirement, but also a smart move for your nonprofit organization. If your website is not accessible, your nonprofit might be missing the chance to connect with millions of people, including potential supporters.

[This free webinar](https://torchbox.zoom.us/webinar/register/9017113651727/WN_axs8fqCnRi6-du0KvaTw8g#/registration) will highlight the importance of accessibility for users with disabilities and the broader benefits of providing a user-friendly experience for all.

**What you will learn:**

- The importance of digital accessibility for nonprofits: beyond compliance, towards inclusivity.
- Insights into the “Digital Inclusivity Dollar” – understanding the impact of accessibility on user engagement and donor outreach.
- A case study from the RNIB: actionable lessons from their website accessibility project.
- Practical tips and recommendations for initiating your organization’s journey towards digital accessibility.
- How to conduct an accessibility audit: identifying areas for improvement and implementing changes.
