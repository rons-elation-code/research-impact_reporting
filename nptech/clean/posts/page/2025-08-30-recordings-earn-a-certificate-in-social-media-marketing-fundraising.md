# [Recordings] Earn a Certificate in Social Media Marketing & Fundraising

> The 2025 Certificate in Social Media Marketing & Fundraising program covers the fundamentals of social media marketing and fundraising for your nonprofit. The certificate program will provide an honest, practical approach to using social media at a time when organic reach and engagement are at an all-time low.

The [2025 Certificate in Social Media Marketing & Fundraising program](https://buy.stripe.com/3csg2u2oZ75Tfy8aEZ) covers the fundamentals of social media marketing and fundraising for your nonprofit. With a focus on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Threads, and Bluesky for nonprofits, participants will learn how to create a social media strategy, craft a content marketing plan, and current best practices for using social media for community engagement and fundraising. - How to create a consistent brand across social media platforms
- How to grow your social media following

**2) Social Media Content Marketing for Nonprofits  
Recorded August 20, 2025 • 2 hours**

- How to create a social media content marketing plan for your nonprofit

- How to create an editorial calendar for your content marketing plan

**3) Social Media Fundraising for Nonprofits

- How to use social media to grow your email and text subscribers
