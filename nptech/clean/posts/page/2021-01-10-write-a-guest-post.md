Want to grow your personal brand within the nonprofit community? Writing a guest post for Nonprofit Tech for Good can definitely do that!

Guest posts must focus on digital marketing and fundraising trends for nonprofits. Content must be focused on nonprofit technology, digital marketing, or online fundraising and our readers are most interested in posts related to social media, online and social fundraising, email marketing, and emerging trends.

Guest posts are solely for bloggers that specialize in the nonprofit sector and nonprofit staff. If you are a for-profit business, please see our [sponsorship opportunities](https://www.nptechforgood.com/sponsor-nonprofit-tech-for-good/).

## **Benefits**

Your guest post is promoted for a minimum of four weeks and when applicable, your social profiles are tagged on social media. In addition, your guest post can include up to 10 [backlinks](https://backlinko.com/hub/seo/backlinks) to your website in order to help improve your website’s SEO and referral traffic from Nonprofit Tech for Good.

Depending upon the popularity of your post, over a 12-month period visitors range from 2,000-4,000 and click-throughs to your website range from 3-10%.

## Where Your Guest Post is Promoted

- **Our Weekly Newsletter****:** Your sponsored post is featured for 5-6 weeks in [our newsletter](https://subscribepage.io/HOjlsE) (12,000 subscribers).
- **Facebook:**Your sponsored post is shared once to our [Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/nonprofitorgs) (110,000 followers) and once to our [Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/nptechforgood) (3,200 members).
- **LinkedIn:** Your sponsored post is shared once to our [LinkedIn Page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/nonprofitorgs) (23,000 followers) and to our LinkedIn Groups (when relevant): [Social Media for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/1172477) (200,000 members), [Online Fundraising for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/3694133) (61,000 members), [Website & Email Marketing for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/12078228/) (3,100 members), [Artificial Intelligence for Nonprofit Organizations](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/14431695/) (1,700 members).
- **Instagram:** Your sponsored post is shared once to our [Instagram Account](https://www.instagram.com/nonprofitorgs/) (77,000 followers).
- **X:**Your sponsored post is posted/reposted to [our X account](https://twitter.com/nonprofitorgs) (740,000 followers) 4-8 times depending upon its popularity.
- **Threads:** Your sponsored post is posted once to our [Threads profile](https://www.threads.net/@nonprofitorgs) (11,000 followers).
- **Bluesky:** Your resource is posted once to our [Bluesky account](https://bsky.app/profile/nonprofitorgs.bsky.social) (3,600 followers).
- **Pinterest:** Your sponsored post is pinned once to our [Pinterest profile](https://www.pinterest.com/nonprofitorgs/) (25,000 followers).

## **Content Guidelines**

- Content must be written in the style of Nonprofit Tech for Good (simple, straightforward, useful). How-to posts with screenshots, numbered lists, and infographics are the posts that perform best.
- Images must be a minimum of 600 pixels in width. No stock photos.
- Posts must be written in the third person.
- Please include a byline for the top of your post and “About the Author” information [for the bottom of your post](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/08/06/your-guide-to-year-end-fundraising-best-practices/).
- You can add a CTA for a guide, webinar, case study, etc. [in the middle of the post](https://www.nptechforgood.com/2023/10/12/5-ways-to-ensure-your-social-media-content-works-in-time-for-giving-tuesday/).
- Content cannot be overly promotional or commercial, however, your sponsored post can include up to [10 backlinks](https://backlinko.com/hub/seo/backlinks) which improves your website’s SEO and referral traffic from Nonprofit Tech for Good. No “click here” or “here” links please.
- Be mindful of SEO. The title of your post and the first paragraph should include important [keywords](https://yoast.com/what-is-a-keyword/) relevant to the primary subject of your post. If possible, include the word “Nonprofit” in your title. To improve SEO, Nonprofit Tech for Good will add a meta description and image alt text to your post.
- Your sponsored post can also be published on your blog, however, please use a different title and introduction paragraph for SEO reasons.
- Please submit your sponsored post as a Word attachment via email. Images should also be submitted via email as attachments in .jpg or .png format.
