As you review your fundraising plans and goals for the past year, it’s easy to look back and see a year marked by postponed and cancelled events. That’s why we’re providing you with free resources for planning, promoting and executing a successful virtual fundraising event. With these nine resources at your disposal, your nonprofit will be primed to meet and exceed your 2021 fundraising goals.
