This allows nonprofits to optimize their fundraising efforts and focus on cultivating the most promising relationships.

## **Tips to Step Up Your Salesforce Game**

- **Supercharge Segmentation with Advanced Analytics:** Go beyond basic demographics: You’re likely already segmenting by basic factors like donation amount or location. Dive deeper! Use Salesforce reports and dashboards to analyze giving patterns, engagement with email campaigns, website interactions, and event attendance.
- **Create dynamic segments:** Don’t just set static lists. Use criteria-based segmentation to automatically update lists as donors act. This ensures your communications are *always* targeted to the right audience at the right time.
- **Unleash the power of Einstein AI:** Predictive scoring with Einstein can analyze your data to predict which donors are most likely to give again, upgrade their donation, or attend an event. This lets you prioritize your outreach efforts for maximum impact.
- **Employ next-best actions:** Get AI-powered recommendations on the most effective engagement strategy for each donor. This could be suggesting a personalized email, a phone call, or inviting them to a specific event.
- **Elevate Your Email Marketing:** Use Salesforce data to personalize email content based on past giving, interests, and engagement history. Go beyond “Dear [Name]” and tailor messaging to resonate with individual donors.
- **Automate journeys:** Create [automated email sequences](https://www.imissioninstitute.org/nonprofit-technology/?utm_source=NPTech4good&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=SF4Fundraisers&utm_id=SF4F25spblog) triggered by donor actions, such as welcome series for new donors, re-engagement campaigns for lapsed donors, or thank-you messages with impact reports.
- **Map the donor lifecycle:** Visualize the stages donors go through from initial awareness to becoming loyal supporters. Identify potential bottlenecks or areas to improve your engagement strategy.
- **Personalize online experiences:** Integrate Salesforce with your website to personalize content, donation forms, and calls to action based on donor profiles.
