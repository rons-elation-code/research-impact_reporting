## **8 AI Concepts and Terminology**

1. **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.
2. **Machine Learning (ML):** A subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data and improve their performance without being explicitly programmed.
3. **Neural Networks:** Algorithms inspired by the human brain that are used in deep learning and other AI applications.
4. **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** The ability of machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language.
5. **Supervised Learning:** ML technique where algorithms learn from labeled data to make predictions or decisions.
6. **Unsupervised Learning:** ML technique where algorithms learn patterns and relationships from unlabeled data.
7. **Deep Learning:** A subset of ML involving neural networks with multiple layers, used for complex pattern recognition.
8. **Data Bias:** Prejudice or skewed results in AI systems due to biased training data.
