The 2022 events season is right around the corner, and the time to make critically important decisions about your fundraising strategy is now.

Virtual events have skyrocketed to the mainstream in the last couple of months, and thanks to their many benefits they offer for fundraisers and supporters alike, they’re here to stay. The online format is ideal for nonprofits seeking to expand their reach and open access to a wider audience of supporters.

But how do you decide if you should host a virtual, in-person, or hybrid event?

[In this webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/269608367227044620), we will dive into what’s working with in-person, virtual, and hybrid events and auctions so you can make the right choice for your organization. We will:

- See new survey data on what small nonprofits believe will be the future of events
- Learn what nonprofits are doing right now to keep donors and prospects engaged while staying safe
- Review best practices for leveraging new events and auctions-related fundraising technology
