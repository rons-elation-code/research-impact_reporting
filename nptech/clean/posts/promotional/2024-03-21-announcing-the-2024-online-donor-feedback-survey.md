# Announcing the 2024 Online Donor Feedback Survey!

> Have you donated online to a nonprofit within the past 12 months? If yes, please share your feedback by taking the 2024 Online Donor Feedback Survey! The survey is 15 short, easy-to-answer questions that require 2-4 minutes to complete — and your responses are 100% anonymous.

Have you donated online to a nonprofit within the past 12 months? If yes, please share your feedback by taking the [2024 Online Donor Feedback Survey](https://s.surveyplanet.com/05ng5p1j)!

The survey is 15 easy-to-answer questions that require 2-4 minutes to complete — and your responses are 100% anonymous.

Your participation is appreciated and will help nonprofits better serve and communicate with their donors.

The survey will be live until we reach 1,000 respondents and then the data will be analyzed and shared with the Nonprofit Tech for Good community. **Thank you for taking the survey!**
