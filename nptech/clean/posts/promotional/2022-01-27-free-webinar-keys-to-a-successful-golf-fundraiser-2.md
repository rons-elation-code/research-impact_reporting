Golf fundraisers do more than just raise money for an organization or cause—they attract high capacity donors and sponsors, raise awareness, expand your donor base, advance important relationships, and more. Golf holds a ton of potential, but a successful golf tournament takes planning, consideration, and the right tools.

[This webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/9139563283224338957) will dive into the essentials of a successful golf fundraiser, gleaned from working with thousands of charity golf tournaments of all types and sizes. You’ll hear what’s worked for organizations who have held successful golf events, including tips for when to start planning, attracting and working with sponsors, asking for donations, promoting the tournament, saving time, and much more.

Whether you’re a first-timer or veteran event planner, this webinar will provide these key takeaways to help lay the foundation for a fun, lucrative golf tournament:

- How golf tournaments accomplish multiple goals
- Ways to maximize fundraising
- A roundup of best practices, tips, and ideas
- Case studies from successful events
- How to leverage technology
- How to get started
