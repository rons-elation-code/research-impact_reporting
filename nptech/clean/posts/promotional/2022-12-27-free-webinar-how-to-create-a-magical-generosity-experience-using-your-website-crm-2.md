Have you ever sat down with someone in the middle of a movie? You don’t know who the characters are or what’s happening, but your friend gets annoyed when you ask questions. It’s a frustrating experience!

This is what donors experience almost every time websites and CRMs don’t work together. The average nonprofit uses five or more different software platforms to create their donor’s journey, making the experience clunky and disjointed. If you want to give your donors a wonderful experience, making their journey as seamless as possible is important—even on your website.

[In this free webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/7322696192019109982), you’ll uncover innovative ways to design a uniquely magical generosity experience for your donors. We’ll cover:

- How to establish your brand story
- The key steps to designing a generosity experience
- How your website and CRM can work together to create a wonderful donor experience
- Practical tactics to easily connect your website and CRM software

Come learn how to design an immersive and personalized generosity experience for all your supporters.
