Sponsorships are at the heart of a golf tournament’s fundraising power. While golfer registrations are an important piece of the golf event revenue puzzle, sponsorships are where nonprofits can get the most return. Whether you’ve never planned a golf tournament before or your long standing event’s revenue has plateaued, an intense focus on sponsorships can significantly boost your fundraiser’s revenue.

Gleaned from GolfStatus’ experience in powering thousands of nonprofit golf fundraisers, this webinar will discuss how nonprofits can leverage their golf tournament’s sponsorships to not only raise more money, but also open doors to broader partnerships, corporate relationships, and potential high capacity donors.

Designed for event planners of all experience levels, [this free webinar](https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/3489635930484237915) will provide real-life examples and actionable next steps to maximizing your tournament’s sponsorships.

**Key Takeaways:**

- How to craft attractive sponsorship packages for your golf event
- Who to target for your golf fundraiser’s sponsorships
- How to make the ask
- Ideas for unique sponsorship options
- How technology makes it simple
