# Announcing the First Edition of the Global Trends in Giving Report!

The report also explores the impact of gender, generation, and ideology upon giving and volunteerism. Based upon the survey results of 4,084 donors worldwide, the **[2017 Global Trends in Giving Report](https://www.funraise.org/giving-report/past-reports)** summarizes donor data across six continents about how online and mobile technology effects giving. The report also explores the impact of gender, generation, and ideology upon giving and volunteerism.

The report is available in English, French and Spanish and is a sister report to the **[2017 Global NGO Online Technology Report](https://funraise.org/techreport-past-reports)**. The data from both reports is meant to help non-governmental organizations (NGOs), non-profit organizations (NPOs), and charities worldwide better understand if they are using technology in the ways that their donors prefer them to and where they need to improve.
