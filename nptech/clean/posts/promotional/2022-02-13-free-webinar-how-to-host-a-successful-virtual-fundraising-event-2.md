A virtual fundraising event can outperform an in-person event if it is well-planned and hosted using secure technology. In this webinar, learn what it takes to make your virtual fundraising event a success from planning, to execution, to follow-up. How can you convince your donor base to join an online event? What does it take to put on a great show for your donors? How can you touch the hearts of your guests even if they are joining from a remote part of the world?

[This webinar](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/1121733178369242380) is designed to deliver an actionable blueprint for fundraising success based on real-life examples of nonprofit virtual fundraisers. Topics include:

- Best practices for hosting virtual fundraisers and how to ensure a successful, engaging event
- The technology needed to stream a virtual fundraiser, from simple to advanced
- Marketing tips to maximize event registration and excitement for your upcoming event
- How to add an online fundraising auction to your event
- How to use your event data to follow-up with donors and attendees to ensure continued success with virtual events
