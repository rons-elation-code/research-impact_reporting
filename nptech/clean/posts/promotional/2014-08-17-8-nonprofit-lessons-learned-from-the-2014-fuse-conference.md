# 8 Nonprofit Lessons Learned from the 2014 FUSE Conference

> Thanks to a partnership with Salsa Labs, Heather Mansfield of Nonprofit Tech for Good was able to report live from the FUSE Conference on August 6-7 in Annandale, VA. Over 250 nonprofits were in attendance represented by staff mostly from the Millennial Generation who actively contributed to the conference hashtag: #FUSEcon2014. Heather also presented two general sessions during which she invited attendees to share via Instagram the most useful piece of advice they gained from attending the conference. The responses from eight attendees can be heard below: 1. Allie @ Virginia Consumer Voices for Healthcare 2. Liz @ Farmer’s Market Coalition 3. Max @ Religious Action
