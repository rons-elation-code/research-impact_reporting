## **UPDATE: This webinar was presented on May 7, 2019. You can view a recording of the webinar on the [MobileCause website](https://go.mobilecause.com/nftg-mc-mobile-messaging-webinar-19-rp).**

With 96% of all Americans owning mobile devices, and 81% of U.S. adults owning smartphones, it’s imperative that nonprofits adapt to meet donors where they are and how they communicate. This webinar will delve into how nonprofits can harness the power of mobile messaging in order to better engage their audience and raise more money for their cause. We will discuss in-depth:

- How to use the immediacy of mobile messaging to capitalize on donors’ passions and interests
- Creating more personal and targeted messaging to inspire higher levels of giving
- How messaging can heighten fundraising events and cultivate fun, meaningful moments for guests
- Using inbound texts to capture information, provide resources, and more
- Enabling donors to easily give anytime, anywhere with text-to-donate capabilities
- Examples you can incorporate into your own communication and fundraising plans
- How Virginia Tech uses mobile messaging in their campaigns and the results
