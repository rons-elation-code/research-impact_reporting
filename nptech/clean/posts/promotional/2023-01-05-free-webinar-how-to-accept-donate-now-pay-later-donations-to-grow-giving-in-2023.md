Donate Now, Pay Later™ was inspired by the widespread acceptance of Buy Now, Pay Later in the consumer retail space. Across generations, nearly 70% of consumers say they prefer Buy Now, Pay Later over credit cards due to the ease of set payments and the lack of interest charges.

In 2022, Donate Now, Pay Later™ started to become adopted by nonprofits, such as PETA and the Jewish Federation, and in 2023 the growth of this new payment method is projected to be significant and exponential.

[**In this free webinar**](https://register.gotowebinar.com/register/6658879241081791576), you’ll learn why Donate Now, and Pay Later™ means more and larger than average donations to your nonprofit. Topics include:

- Why Gen Z and Millennial donors prefer Donate Now, Pay Later™
- An examination of why Donate Now, Pay Later™ donors give more (270% more on average)
- How the Donate Now, Pay Later™ transaction process works for nonprofits and donors
- How to add a Donate Now, Pay Later™ to your nonprofit’s donation page
- How to promote Donate Now, Pay Later™ to your nonprofit’s donors and supporters
