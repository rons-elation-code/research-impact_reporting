# New! 2024 Certificate in Digital Marketing & Fundraising for Nonprofit Professionals

> Customized for small nonprofits with limited time and financial resources, the 2024 Certificate in Digital Marketing & Fundraising program covers the fundamentals of website design, email marketing, online fundraising, and social media for nonprofits.

Customized for small nonprofits with limited time and financial resources, the **2024 Certificate in Digital Marketing & Fundraising** program covers the fundamentals of website design, email marketing, online fundraising, and social media for nonprofits. Based on [*101 Digital Marketing & Fundraising Best Practices for Nonprofits*](http://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/), the certificate program provides six hours of training and costs $100 USD.

**The required three webinars are:**

**[$30] March 27:**[Website & Email Marketing for Nonprofits](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2020/05/10/webinar-website-email-marketing-best-practices-for-nonprofits/) **[$30] April 3:**[Online Fundraising for Nonprofits](http://www.nptechforgood.com/webinar-online-fundraising-best-practices-for-nonprofits/) **[$40] April 10:**[Social Media for](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2020/06/17/webinar-social-media-best-practices-for-nonprofits/)[Nonprofits](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2020/06/17/webinar-social-media-best-practices-for-nonprofits/)

To earn the 2024 certificate, you can attend the webinars live or view the recordings – or a combination of both. A link to the recording and a comprehensive set of notes is sent within 24 hours after each webinar is over.

**What’s New for 2024:**

- All new webinar content
- New certificate design
- In addition to a PDF, 2024 certificates will also be provided in a web-hosted image format for easy sharing on social media.

**To earn and claim your certificate:**

1. View all three webinars (6 hours of training).
2. [Request your certificate via email from Heather Mansfield](https://www.nptechforgood.com/heather-mansfield/). Please include the passphrase presented in each webinar and how you would like your name featured on the certificate.
3. Within 3-4 business days, you will be sent a PDF of your certificate for printing and a link to your certificate in image format for [easy sharing on LinkedIn](https://www.nptechforgood.com/certificate-programs/how-to-add-nonprofit-tech-for-good-certificates-to-your-linkedin-profile/) and other social media.

If you have already completed the [2024 Certificate in Social Media Marketing & Fundraising](https://www.nptechforgood.com/101-best-practices/earn-a-certificate-in-social-media-marketing-fundraising-from-nonprofit-tech-for-good/), you only need to register for the [Website & Email Marketing](http://www.nptechforgood.com/2020/05/10/webinar-website-email-marketing-best-practices-for-nonprofits/) and the [Online Fundraising](http://www.nptechforgood.com/webinar-online-fundraising-best-practices-for-nonprofits/) webinars to earn the Certificate in Digital Marketing & Fundraising.
