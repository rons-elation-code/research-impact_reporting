# [FREE WEBINAR] How Nonprofits Can Use Crowdfunding to Raise More Money

> UPDATE: This webinar was presented on November 10, 2020. You can view a recording on GoFundMe Charity’s website. GoFundMe Charity will discuss how to run a successful crowdfunding campaign, share their experience in peer-to-peer fundraising, and discuss current fundraising trends in the nonprofit sector. You will learn how to set up a campaign on the GoFundMe Charity platform, the power of social sharing, and see examples of successful crowdfunding campaigns. Additional topics include: How to increase the reach of your crowdfunding campaigns through multi-channel marketing How to increase engagement How to make your donor data actionable
